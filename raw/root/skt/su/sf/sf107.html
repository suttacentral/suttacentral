Daśottarasūtra

evaṃ maya śrutam | ekasmin samaye …

dharmaṃ deśayiṣyāmy ādau kalyāṇaṃ madhye kalyāṇaṃ paryavasāne kalyāṇaṃ svarthaṃ suvyañjanaṃ kevalaṃ paripūrṇaṃ pariśuddhaṃ paryavadātam | brahamacaryaṃ prakāśayiṣyate yaduta daśottaro nāma dharmaparyāyaḥ |

… śṛṇuta sādhu ca suṣṭhu ca manasikuruta | bhāṣiṣye daśottaraṃ nāma dharmaparyāyaṃ nirvāṇagāminaṃ duḥkhasyāntakriyāyai ca sarvagranthapramocanam | daśottaro nāma dharmaparyāyaḥ katamaḥ |

1

eko dharmo bahukaro yaduta | apramādaḥ kuśaleṣu dharmeṣu |

eko dharmo bhāvayitavyaḥ | kāyagatā smṛtiḥ śātasahagatā |

eko dharmaḥ parijñeyaḥ | sparśaḥ sāsrava u­pād­ānīyaḥ |

eko dharmaḥ prahātavyaḥ | asmimānaḥ |

eko dharmo hānabhāgīyaḥ | ayoniśo manasikāraḥ |

eko dharmo viśeṣabhāgīyaḥ | yoniśo manasikāraḥ |

eko dharmo duṣprativedhaḥ | ānantaryacetaḥ samādhiḥ |

eko dharma utpādayitavyaḥ | asāmayikī …

eko dharmo ’bhijñeyaḥ | sarvasattvā āhārasthitayaḥ |

eko dharmaḥ sākṣīkartavyaḥ | akopyā cetovimuktiḥ |

itīma āyuṣmanto daśa dharmās tathā avitathā ananyathā bhūtāḥ satyās tathayā yathābhūtā aviparītā aviparyastā evam etad yathābhūtaṃ samyakprajñayā draṣṭavyāḥ ||

2

dvau dharmau bahakarau | smṛtiś ca saṃprajanyaṃ ca |

dvau dharmau bhāvayitavyau | śamathaś ca vipaśya nā ca |

dvau dharmau parijñeyau | nāmaṃ ca rūpaṃ ca |

dvau dharmau prahātavyau | avidyā ca bhavatṛṣṇā ca |

dvau dharmau hānabhāgīyau | āhrīkyam anavatrāpyaṃ ca |

dvau dharmau viśeṣabhāgīyau | hrīś ca vyavatrāpyaṃ ca |

dvau dharmau duṣprativedhau | sthānaṃ ca sthānato duṣprativedham asthānaṃ cāsthānataḥ |

dvau dharmāv utpādayitavyau | kṣayajñānam anutpādajñānañ ca |

dvau dharmāv abhijñeyau | yaś ca hetur yaḥ pratyayaḥ sattvānāṃ saṃkleśāya | yaś ca hetur yaḥ pratyayaḥ sattvānāṃ viśuddhaye |

dvau dharmau sākṣīkartavyau | vidyā ca vimuktiś ca |

itīma āyuṣmanto viṃśatir dharmās tathā avitathāḥ pūrvavad yāvat samyakprajñayā draṣṭavyāḥ ||

3

trayo dharmā bahukarāḥ | satpuruṣa­saṃ­sevaḥ saddharmaśravaṇaṃ yoniśo manasikāraḥ |

trayo dharmā bhāvayitavyāḥ | trayaḥ samādhayaḥ | savitarkaḥ savicāraḥ samādhir avitarko vicāramātraḥ samādhir avitarko ’vicāraḥ samādhiḥ |

trayo dharmāḥ parijñeyāḥ | trayo bhavāḥ | kāmabhavo rūpabhava ārūpyabhavaḥ |

trayo dharmāḥ prahātavyāḥ | tisras tṛṣṇāḥ | kāmatṛṣṇā rūpatṛṣṇā ārūpyatṛṣṇā |

trayo dharmā hānabhāgīyāḥ | trīṇy akuśalamūlāni | lobho ’kuśalamūlaṃ dveṣo moho ’kuśalamūlam |

trayo dharmā viśeṣabhāgīyāḥ | trīṇi kuśalamūlāni | alobhaḥ kuśalamūlam adveṣo ’mohaḥ kuśalamūlam |

trayo dharmā duṣprativedhāḥ | trīṇi nimittāni | samādhinimittaṃ samādhisthitinimittaṃ samādhivyutthānanimittam |

trayo dharmā utpādayitavyāḥ | trīṇi vimokṣamukhāni | śūnyatā apraṇihitam ānimittam |

trayo dharmā abhijñeyāḥ | tisro vedanāḥ | sukhā duḥkhā aduḥkhāsukhāś ca |

trayo dharmāḥ sākṣīkartavyāḥ | tisro ’śaikṣyo vidyāḥ | katamās tisraḥ | aśaikṣī pūrve nivāsānusmṛtij­ñāna­sākṣīkriyā vidyā | aśaikṣī cyutyupa­pād­aj­ñāna­sākṣīkriyā vidyā | aśaikṣy āsravakṣayaj­ñāna­sākṣīkriyā vidyā |

itīma āyuṣmantas triṃśad dharmāḥ pūrvavat |

4

catvāro dharmā bahukarāḥ | catvāri devamanuṣyāṇāṃ cakrāṇi yair deva …

yamānā vṛddhiṃ vaipulyam āpadyante kuśalair dharmaiḥ | katamāni catvāri |

pratirūpo deśāvāsaḥ satpuruṣāpāśraya ātmanaś ca samyakpraṇidhānaṃ pūrve ca kṛtapuṇyatā |

catvāro dharmā bhāvayitavyāḥ | catvāri smṛtyupasthānāni | katamāni catvāri | kāye kāyānupaśyanā smṛtyupasthānam | vedanāyāṃ citte dharmeṣu dharmānupaśyanā smṛtyupasthānam |

catvāro dharmāḥ parijñeyāḥ | catvāra āhārāḥ | kabaḍiṃ­kāra āhāra audārikaḥ sūkṣmaś ca | sparśo dvitīyo manaḥsañcetanā tṛtīyā vijñānaṃ caturtham |

catvāro dharmāḥ prahātavyāḥ | catvāry upādānāni | katamāni catvāri | kāmopādānaṃ dṛṣṭyupādānaṃ śīlavratopādānam ātmavādopādānam |

catvāro dharmā hānabhāgīyāḥ | catasro vipattayaḥ | śīlavipattir dṛṣṭivipattir ācāravipattir ājīvavipattiḥ |

catvāro dharmā viśeṣabhāgīyāḥ | catasro saṃpattayaḥ | śīlasaṃpattir dṛṣṭisaṃpattir ācārasaṃpattir ājīvasaṃpattiḥ |

catvāro dharmā duṣprativedhāḥ | catvāry āryasatyāni | katamāni catvāri | duḥkham āryasatyaṃ duḥkhasamudayo duḥkhanirodho duḥkha­nirodhagāminī pratipad āryasatyam |

catvāro dharmā utpādayitavyāḥ | catvāri jñānāni | duḥkhajñānaṃ samudayajñānaṃ nirodhajñānaṃ mārgajñānam |

catvāro dharmā abhijñeyāḥ | catasraḥ saṃjñāḥ | parittam eke saṃjānaṃti | mahadgatam eke saṃjānaṃti | apramāṇam eke saṃjānaṃti | nāsti kiñcid ity ākiñcanyāyatanam eke saṃjānaṃti |

catvāro dharmāḥ sākṣīkartavyāḥ | catvāraḥ sākṣīkaraṇīyā dharmāḥ | santi dharmāḥ kāyena sākṣīkartavyāḥ santi smṛtyā santi cakṣuṣā santi prajñayā sākṣīkartavyā dharmāḥ |

itīma āyuṣmanto catvāriṃśad dharmās tathā avitathāḥ | pūrvavad yāvat samyakprajñayā draṣṭavyāḥ |

5

pañca dharmā bahukarāḥ | pañca prādhānikasyāṅgāni |

iha bhikṣos tathāgatasyāntike śraddhābhinirviṣṭā bhavati mūlajātā pratiṣṭhitā …

dharmataḥ | idam prathamaṃ prādhānikasyāṅgam |

punar aparaṃ bhikṣuḥ …

… sa­brahma­cāriṇām | idaṃ dvitīyam prādhānikasyāṅgam |

punar aparaṃ bhikṣur alpābādho bhavaty arogajātīyaḥ samavipacanayā grahaṇyā samanvāgato nātyuṣṇayā nātiśītayāvyābādhayartusukhayā yayāsyāśitapītakhāditāni samyaksukhena paripākaṃ gacchanti | idaṃ tṛtīyaṃ prādhānikasyāṅgam |

punar aparaṃ bhikṣuḥ …

… sthāmavān … dṛḍhaparākramo ’nikṣiptadhuraḥ kuśaleṣu dharmeṣu | idaṃ caturthaṃ prādhānikasyāṅgam |

5.2

pañca dharmāḥ parijñeyāḥ | pañcopādānaskandhāḥ | katame pañca | rūpopādānaskandho vedanā saṃjñā saṃskārā vijñānam |

pañca dharmāḥ prahātavyāḥ | pañca nīvaraṇāni | katamāni pañca | kāmac­chandanīvaraṇam | vyāpādaḥ styāna­middham auddhatyakaukṛtyaṃ vicikitsā |

pañca dharmā hānabhāgīyāḥ | pañca cetaḥkhilāḥ | katame pañca | ihāyuṣmanto bhikṣuḥ śāstari kāṅkṣati vicikitsati na … nādhimucyate na saṃprasīdati …

ś ca vijñaiḥ sabrahmacāribhis tān vākpratodakair vigarhaty ? aṅgulipratodakaiś ca ? …

… kupitaḥ ? sabrahmacāriṣu |

pañca dharmā viśeṣabhāgīyāḥ | pañcendriyāṇi | katamāni pañca | śraddhendriyaṃ vīryendriyaṃ smṛtīndriyaṃ samādhīndriyaṃ prajñendriyam |

pañca dharmā duṣprativedhāḥ | pañca niḥsaraṇīyā dhātavaḥ |

tasya khalv āyuṣmantaḥ śrutavata āryaśrāvakasya …

… kāmān anusmarataḥ kāmeṣu cittaṃ na praskandati na prasīdati na santiṣṭhate na vimucyate pratilīyate pratiput …

… snāyugardulakaṃ vāgnāv upasaṃhṛtaṃ pratilīyate na pra …

… kāmān anusmarataḥ kāmeṣu cittaṃ na praskandati na prasīdati na santiṣṭhate na vimucyate pratilīyate …

na sa tadvedanāṃ vedayati | idaṃ niḥsaraṇam

yathā kāmānām evaṃ vyāpādo ’vyāpādo vihiṃsāvihiṃsā rūpam ārūpyaṃ satkāyaḥ satkāyanirodho ’sti niḥsaraṇaṃ |

pañca dharmā utpādayitavyāḥ | āryaḥ pañcajñānikaḥ samyaksamādhiḥ |

ayaṃ samādhir āryo nirāmiṣo niraupadhika ity evam asya prathamaṃ jñānam adhyātmam utpadyamānam utpadyate |

ayaṃ samādhir akāpuruṣasevito vijñapraśasto ’garhito vijñaiḥ sabrahmacaribhir evam asya dvitīyaṃ jñānam adhyātama utpadyamānam utpadyate |

ayaṃ samādhiḥ śāntaḥ praṇītaḥ pratiprasrabdhilabdha ekotībhāvādhigata ity evam asya trtīyaṃ jñānam adhyātmam utpadyamānam utpadyate |

ayaṃ samādhiḥ pratyutpannasukha āyatyāṃ sukhavipākaś cety evam asya caturthaṃ jñānam adhyātmam utpadyamānam utpadyate |

punaḥ khalu samādhiṃ smṛta eva samāpatsyāmi smṛta eva vyutthāsyāmīty evam asya pañcamaṃ jñānam adhyātmam utpadyamānam utpadyate |

|| antaroddānaṃ ||
āryo ’kāpuruṣaḥ śāntaḥ
pratyutpannasmṛtena ca |

pañca dharmā abhijñeyāḥ | pañca vimuktyāyatanāni | katamāni pañca |

iha bhikṣor śāstā vā dharmān deśayaty anyatamānyatamo vā vijño gurusthānīyaḥ sabrahmacārī

yathā yathāsya śāstā dharmān deśayaty anyatamānyatamo vā vijño guru­sthānīyaḥ sabrahmacārī tathā tathā sa teṣu dharmeṣv arthapratisaṃvedī bhavati dharmapratisaṃvedī ca |

ta syārthapratisaṃvedino dharmapratisaṃvedina utpadyate prāmodyaṃ pramuditasya prītir jāyate prītamanasaḥ kāyaḥ prasrabhyate prasrabdhakāyaḥ sukhaṃ vetti sukhitasya cittaṃ samādhīyate | idaṃ prathamaṃ vimuktyāyatanam |

yatra sthitasya bhikṣor vā bhikṣuṇyā vāvimuktaṃ cittaṃ vimucyate ’samāhitaṃ cittaṃ samādhīyate ’parikṣīṇā āsravāḥ parikṣīyante ’nanuprāptaṃ cottare yogakṣemaṃ nirvāṇaṃ anuprāpnoti |

punar iha bhikṣor naiva śāstā vā dharmāñ deśayaty anyatamānyatamo vā vijño gurusthānīyaḥ sabrahamcāry api ca sa yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān vistareṇa svareṇa svādhyāyaṃ karoti

yathā yathā sa yathā śrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān vistareṇa svareṇa svādhyāyaṃ karoti | tathā tathā sa teṣu dharmeṣv arthapratisaṃvedī bhavati dharmapratisaṃvedī ca | tasyārthapratisaṃ­vedino dharmapratisaṃvedina utpadyate prāmodyaṃ pūrvavad idaṃ dvitīyaṃ vimuktyāyatanam |

punar aparaṃ nāpy asya śāstā dharmān deśayaty anyatamānyatamo vā vijño gurusthānīyaḥ sabrahmacārī nāpi yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān vistareṇa svareṇa svādhyāyaṃ karoty api ca sa yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān vistareṇa pareṣāṃ saṃprakāśayati

yathā yathā sa yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān vistareṇa pareṣāṃ saṃprakāśayati tathā tathā sa teṣu dharmeṣv arthapratisaṃvedī bhavati dharmapratisaṃvedī ca | tasyārthaprati­saṃvedino dharmaprati­saṃ­vedina utpadyate prāmodyaṃ pūrvavad idaṃ tṛtīyaṃ vimuktyāyatanam |

punar aparaṃ nāpy asya śāstā dharmān deśayaty anyatamānyatamo vā vijño gurusthānīyaḥ sabrahmacāri nāpi yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān vistareṇa svareṇa svādhyāyaṃ karoti nāpi yathā­śrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān vistareṇa pareṣaṃ saṃprakāśyaty api ca yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān ekākī rohagataś cintayati tulayaty upaparīkṣate

yathā yathā khalu yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān ekākī rohagataś cintayati tulayati saṃparīkṣate tathā tathā sa teṣu dharmeṣv arthapratisaṃvedī bhavati dharmapratisaṃvedī ca | tasyārthaprati­saṃ­vedino dharmapratisaṃvedina utpadyate prāmodyaṃ pūrvavad idaṃ caturthaṃ vimuktyāyatanam |

punar aparaṃ nāpy asya śāstā dharmān deśayati nāpy anyatamānyatamo vijño gurustānīyaḥ sabrahmacārī nāpi yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān vistareṇa svareṇa svādhyāyaṃ karoti nāpi yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān vistareṇa pareṣāṃ saṃprakāśayati nāpi yathāśrutān yathāparyāptān dharmān ekākī rahogataś cintayati tulayaty upaparīkṣate ’ pi ca khalv asya bhikṣor anyataraṃ samādhinimittaṃ bhavati sugṛhītaṃ sumanasikṛtaṃ supradhāritaṃ supratividdhaṃ |

yathā yathā khalv asya bhikṣor anyataraṃ samādhinimittaṃ bhavati sugṛhītaṃ sumanasikṛtaṃ supradhāritaṃ supratividdhaṃ tathā tathā sa teṣu dharmeṣv arthapratisaṃvedī bhavati dharmapratisaṃvedī ca |

tasyārthaprati­saṃ­vedino dharmaprati­saṃ­vedina utpadyate prāmodyaṃ pramudityasya prītir jāyate prītamanasaḥ kāyaḥ prasrabhyate prasrabdhakāyaḥ sukhaṃ vetti sukhitasya cittaṃ samādhīyate | idaṃ pañcamaṃ vimuktyā­yatanam |

yatra sthitasya bhikṣor bhikṣuṇyā vāvimuktaṃ cittaṃ vimucyate ’samāhitaṃ cittaṃ samādhīyate ’parikṣīṇā āsravāḥ parikṣīyante ’nanuprāptaṃ cottare yogakṣemaṃ nirvāṇam anuprāpnoti |

pañca dharmāḥ sākṣīkartavyāḥ | pañca dharma­skandhāḥ | āryaḥ śīlaskandhaḥ samādhiskandhaḥ prajñāskandho vimuktiskandho vimuktijñānadarśanaskandhaḥ |

itīma āyuṣmantaḥ pañcāśad dharmās tathā avitathāḥ pūrvavat |

6

ṣaḍ dharmā bhhukarāḥ | ṣaṭ saṃraṃjanīyā dharmāḥ | katame ṣaṭ |

maitraṃ me kāyakarma pratyupasthitaṃ bhaviṣyati śāstur antike vijñānāñ ca sabrahmacāriṇāṃ |

ayaṃ dharmaḥ saṃraṃjanīyaḥ priyakaraṇo gurukaraṇo manāpakaranaḥ priyatvāya gurutvāya gauravāya bhāvanāya saṃgrahāya samādhaye ’vigrahāyāvivādāya ekotībhāvāya saṃvartate |

maitraṃ vākkarma |

maitraṃ manaskarma |

ye te lābhā dhārmikā dharmalabdhā antataḥ pātragatāḥ pātraparyāpannās tadrūpeṣu lābheṣu sādhāraṇaparibhojino bhaviṣyāmo ’pratiguptabhojinaḥ sārdhaṃ vijñaiḥ sabrahmacāribhiḥ | ayaṃ dharmaḥ saṃraṃjanīyaḥ pūrvavat |

yāni tāni śīlāny akhaṇḍāny acchidrāṇy aśabalāny akalmāṣāṇi bhujiṣyāṇy aparāmṛṣṭāni susamāptāni susamādattāni vijñapraśastāny agarhitāni vijñais

tadrūpaiḥ śīlaiḥ śīlasāmānyagatā bhaviṣyāmaḥ sārdham vijñaiḥ sabrahmacāribhiḥ | ayaṃ dharmaḥ saṃraṃjanīyaḥ pūrvavat |

yeyaṃ dṛṣṭir āryā nairyāṇikī nairvedhikī niryāti tatkaraḥ samyagduḥkhakṣayāya duḥkhasyāntakriyāyai tadrūpayā dṛṣṭyā dṛṣṭisāmānyagatā bhaviṣyāmaḥ sārdhaṃ vijñaiḥ sabrahmacāribhiḥ | ayaṃ dharmaḥ saṃraṃjanīyaḥ pūrvavad yāvad ekotībhāvāya saṃvartate |

ṣaḍ dharmā bhāvayitavyāḥ | ṣaṭ sātatavihārāḥ | katame ṣaṭ |

iha bhikṣuś cakṣuṣā rūpāṇi dṛṣṭvā naiva sumanā bhavati na durmanā upekṣako viharati smṛtaḥ saṃprajānaḥ |

śrotreṇa śabdān ghrāṇena gandhāñ jihvayā rasān kāyena spraṣṭavyān manasā dharmān vijñāya naiva sumanā bhavati na durmanā upekṣako viharati smṛtaḥ saṃprajānaḥ |

ṣaḍ dharmāḥ parijñeyāḥ | ṣaḍ ādhyātmikāny āyatanāni | katamāni ṣaṭ | cakṣur ādhyātmikam āyatanaṃ śrotraṃ ghrāṇaṃ jihvā kāyo mana ādhyātmikam āyatanam |

ṣaḍ dharmāḥ prahātavyāḥ | ṣaṭ tṛṣṇākāyāḥ | katame ṣaṭ | cakṣuḥsaṃsparśajā tṛṣṇa śrotraghrāṇajihvākāyamanaḥ­saṃsparśajā tṛṣṇā |

ṣaḍ dharmā hānabhāgīyāḥ | ṣaḍ agauravatāḥ | katamāḥ ṣaṭ | buddhe ’gauravo viharati … pāpamitraś ca bhavati |

ṣaḍ dharmā viśeṣabhāgīyāḥ | ṣaḍ gauravatāḥ pūrvavat |

ṣaḍ dharmā duṣprativedhāḥ | ṣaḍ niḥsaraṇīyā dhātavaḥ | katame ṣaṭ |

ihāyuṣmanto bhikṣur evaṃ vadet | maitro me cetaḥsamādhir āsevito bhāvito bahulīkṛto ’tha ca punar me vyāpādaś cittaṃ paryādāya tiṣṭhati |

maivaṃ voca iti syād vacanīyaḥ | tat kasmād dhetoḥ | asthānam anavakāśo yan maitre cetaḥsamādhāv āsevite bhāvite bahulīkṛte vyāpādaś cittaṃ paryādāya sthāsyati | nedaṃ sthānaṃ vidyate |

niḥsaraṇam idaṃ sarvavyāpādānāṃ yaduta maitraś cetaḥsamādhiḥ |

iha ya āyuṣmanta evaṃ vadet | karuṇo me cetaḥsamādhir āsevito bhāvito bahulīkṛto ’tha ca punar me vihiṃsā cittaṃ paryādāya tiṣṭhati |

maivaṃ voca iti syād vacanīyaḥ |tat kasmād dhetoḥ | asthānam anavakāśo yat karuṇe cetaḥsamādhāv āsevite bhāvite bahulīkṛte vihiṃsā cittaṃ paryādāya sthāsyati | nedaṃ sthānaṃ vidyate |
