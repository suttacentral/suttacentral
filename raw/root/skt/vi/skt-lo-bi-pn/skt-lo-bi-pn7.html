bhikṣuṇī-prakirṇaka 7

veśyā-pratisaṁyuktaṁ

Origin story

bhagavān śrāvastīyam viharati | tāyo dāni bhikṣuṇīyo śākīya-kanyāyo koliya-kanyāyo malla-kanyāyo mahā-kulehi pravrajitā ceṭikām ādāya pravrajanti | tāyo ceṭikāyo prāsādikāyo darśanīyāyo veśyaṁ vāhayanti | jano dani ojjhāyati | neyaṁ pravrajyā gaṇikā imā | etaṁ prakaraṇaṁ bhikṣuṇīhi śrutaṁ | yāvad āma bhagavan | bhagavān āha | evaṁ ca nāma yūyaṁ veśyām upasthāpayatha |

The ruling

tena hi na kṣamati veśyām upasthāpayituṁ |

Cases

sā eṣā bhikṣuṇī veśyām upasthāpayati tena jīvikāṁ kalpayati vinayātikramam āsādayati | idam ucyate veśyā-pratisaṁyuktaṁ || grā ||
