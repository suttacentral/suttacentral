29. The Buddha in Veśālī

atha bhagavān anupūrveṇa vaiśālīm anuprāptaḥ || bhagavāṃ dāni vaiśālīye sābhyantarabāhirāye svastyayanaṃ karoti | svastyayanagāthāṃ bhāṣati ||
