Saṃyutta Nikāya 6

1. Paṭhamavagga

8. Kata­modaka­tissa­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā divāvihāragato hoti paṭisallīno. Atha kho subrahmā ca paccekabrahmā suddhāvāso ca paccekabrahmā yena bhagavā tenu­pasaṅka­miṃsu; upasaṅkamitvā paccekaṃ dvārabāhaṃ nissāya aṭṭhaṃsu. Atha kho suddhāvāso paccekabrahmā kata­modaka­tis­sakaṃ bhikkhuṃ ārabbha bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi:

“Appameyyaṃ paminanto,
Kodha vidvā vikappaye;
Appameyyaṃ pamāyinaṃ,
Nivutaṃ taṃ maññe akissavan”ti.
