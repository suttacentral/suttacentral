Saṃyutta Nikāya 32

1. Valāhakavagga

1. Suddhikasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Valāhakakāyike vo, bhikkhave, deve desessāmi. Taṃ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, valāhakakāyikā devā? Santi, bhikkhave, sītavalāhakā devā; santi uṇhavalāhakā devā; santi abbhavalāhakā devā; santi vātavalāhakā devā; santi vassavalāhakā devā—ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, ‘valāhakakāyikā devā’”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
