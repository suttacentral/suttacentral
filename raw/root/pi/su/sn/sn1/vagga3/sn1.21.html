Saṃyutta Nikāya 1

3. Sattivagga

21. Sattisutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi: 

“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,
ḍayhamānova matthake;
­Kāmarā­gap­pahānāya,
sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.

“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,
ḍayhamānova matthake;
Sakkā­ya­diṭṭhip­pahānāya,
sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.
