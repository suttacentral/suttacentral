Saṃyutta Nikāya 47

3. Sīlaṭṭhi­tivagga

27. Samattasutta

Taṃyeva nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho āyasmā sāriputto āyasmantaṃ anuruddhaṃ etadavoca: “‘asekho, asekho’ti, āvuso anuruddha, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, āvuso, asekho hotī”ti? “Catunnaṃ kho, āvuso, sati­paṭṭhā­nā­naṃ samattaṃ bhāvitattā asekho hoti.

Katamesaṃ catunnaṃ? Idhāvuso, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhij­jhā­do­manas­saṃ; vedanāsu … pe … citte … pe … dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhij­jhā­do­manas­saṃ. Imesaṃ kho, āvuso, catunnaṃ sati­paṭṭhā­nā­naṃ samattaṃ bhāvitattā asekho hotī”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
