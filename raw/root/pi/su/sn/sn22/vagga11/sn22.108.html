Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

11. Antavagga

108. Duti­yasama­ṇa­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Pañcime, bhikkhave, upādā­nak­khan­dhā. Katame pañca? Seyyathidaṃ—rūpupā­dā­nak­khan­dho, vedanupā­dā­nak­khan­dho, saññu­pādā­nak­khan­dho, saṅ­khā­ru­pādā­nak­khan­dho, viñ­ñāṇupā­dā­nak­khan­dho. Ye hi keci, bhikkhave, samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā imesaṃ pañcannaṃ upādā­nak­khan­dhā­naṃ samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ nappajānanti … pe … pajānanti, sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharantī”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
