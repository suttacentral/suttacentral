Saṃyutta Nikāya 24

1. Sotāpattivagga

17. Hoti­cana­cahoti­tathā­gato­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati: ‘hoti ca na ca hoti tathāgato paraṃ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā … pe … niyato sam­bodhi­parā­yano”ti.

Sattarasamaṃ.
