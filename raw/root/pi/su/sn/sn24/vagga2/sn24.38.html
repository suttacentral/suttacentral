Saṃyutta Nikāya 24

2. Duti­yagama­na­vagga

38. Arūpīattāsutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati: ‘arūpī attā hoti arogo paraṃ maraṇā’”ti? (Peyyālo.)

Vīsatimaṃ.
