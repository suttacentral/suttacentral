Saṃyutta Nikāya 48

1. Suddhikavagga

1. Suddhikasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Tatra kho bhagavā etadavoca. “ Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ, paññindriyaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
