Saṃyutta Nikāya 48

4. Sukhin­dri­yavagga

31. Suddhikasutta

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Sukhindriyaṃ, dukkhindriyaṃ, somanas­sindriyaṃ, domanas­sindriyaṃ, upekkhindriyaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
