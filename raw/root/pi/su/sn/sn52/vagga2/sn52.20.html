Saṃyutta Nikāya 52

2. Dutiyavagga

20. Indriya­paro­pariyat­tasutta

“Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ sati­paṭṭhā­nā­naṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā parasattānaṃ parapuggalānaṃ indriya­paro­pariyat­taṃ yathābhūtaṃ pajānāmī”ti.

Dasamaṃ.
