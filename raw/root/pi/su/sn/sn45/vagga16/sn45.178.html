Saṃyutta Nikāya 45

14. Oghavagga

178. Upādā­nak­khan­dha­sutta

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, upādā­nak­khan­dhā. Katame pañca? Seyyathidaṃ— rūpupā­dā­nak­khan­dho, vedanupā­dā­nak­khan­dho, saññu­pādā­nak­khan­dho, saṅ­khā­ru­pādā­nak­khan­dho, viñ­ñāṇupā­dā­nak­khan­dho. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañcu­pādā­nak­khan­dhā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ upādā­nak­khan­dhā­naṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya … pe … ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
