Saṃyutta Nikāya 45

14. Oghavagga

171. Oghasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Cattārome, bhikkhave, oghā. Katame cattāro? Kāmogho, bhavogho, diṭṭhogho, avijjogho—ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro oghā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ oghānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya … pe … ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti. (Yathā esanā, evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

Paṭhamaṃ.
