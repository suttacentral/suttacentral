Saṃyutta Nikāya 45

3. Micchattavagga

25. Paṭha­ma­asap­purisa­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Asappurisañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sappurisañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asappuriso? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco micchādiṭṭhiko hoti, micchāsaṅkappo, micchāvāco, micchākammanto, micchāājīvo, micchāvāyāmo, micchāsati, micchāsamādhi—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asappuriso.

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sappuriso? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco sammādiṭṭhiko hoti, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvāco, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhi—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sappuriso”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
