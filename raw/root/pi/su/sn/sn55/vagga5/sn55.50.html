Saṃyutta Nikāya 55

5. Sagātha­ka­puññā­bhi­sanda­vagga

50. Aṅgasutta

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, sotā­patti­yaṅgāni. Katamāni cattāri? Sap­purisa­saṃ­sevo, saddham­mas­savanaṃ, yoniso­ma­nasikāro, dhammā­nu­dhammap­paṭi­patti—imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri sotā­patti­yaṅgānī”ti.

Dasamaṃ.

Sagātha­ka­puññā­bhi­sanda­vaggo pañcamo.

Abhisandā tayo vuttā,
Duve mahaddhanena ca;
Suddhaṃ nandiyaṃ bhaddiyaṃ,
Mahānāmaṅgena te dasāti.
