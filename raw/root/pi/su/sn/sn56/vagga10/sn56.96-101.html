Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

10. Catut­tha­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vagga

96–​101. Chedanādisutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye cheda­na­va­dha­bandha­na­vi­parā­mo­sa­ālo­pa­saha­sā­kārā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye cheda­na­va­dha­bandha­na­vi­parā­mo­sa­ālo­pa­saha­sā­kārā appaṭiviratā. Taṃ kissa hetu? Adiṭṭhattā bhikkhave, catunnaṃ ariyasaccānaṃ. Katamesaṃ catunnaṃ? Dukkhassa ariyasaccassa … pe … duk­kha­nirodha­gāminiyā paṭipadāya ariyasaccassa.

Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, ‘idaṃ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo … pe … ‘ayaṃ duk­kha­nirodha­gāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.

Ekādasamaṃ.

Catut­tha­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vaggo dasamo.

Khettaṃ kāyaṃ dūteyyañca,
tulākūṭaṃ ukkoṭanaṃ;
Chedanaṃ vadhabandhanaṃ,
viparālopaṃ sāhasanti.
