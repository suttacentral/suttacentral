Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

3. Koṭigāmavagga

28. Lokasutta

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, ariyasaccāni. Katamāni cattāri? Dukkhaṃ ariyasaccaṃ, duk­kha­sa­muda­yaṃ ariyasaccaṃ, dukkhanirodhaṃ ariyasaccaṃ, duk­kha­nirodha­gāminī paṭipadā ariyasaccaṃ. Sadevake loke samārake sabrahmake ­sassama­ṇab­rāhma­ṇiyā pajāya sade­va­manus­sāya tathāgato ariyo; tasmā ‘ariyasaccānī’ti vuccanti.

Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, ‘idaṃ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo … pe … ‘ayaṃ duk­kha­nirodha­gāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
