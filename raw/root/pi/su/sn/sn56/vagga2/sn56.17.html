Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

2. Dhamma­cakkap­pa­vat­ta­na­vagga

17. Avijjāsutta

Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca: “‘avijjā, avijjā’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamā nu kho, bhante, avijjā; kittāvatā ca avijjāgato hotī”ti? “Yaṃ kho, bhikkhu, dukkhe aññāṇaṃ, dukkhasamudaye aññāṇaṃ, dukkhanirodhe aññāṇaṃ, duk­kha­nirodha­gāminiyā paṭipadāya aññāṇaṃ—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhu, avijjā; ettāvatā ca avijjāgato hotīti.

Tasmātiha, bhikkhu, ‘idaṃ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo … pe … ‘ayaṃ duk­kha­nirodha­gāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
