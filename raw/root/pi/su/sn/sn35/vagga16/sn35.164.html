Saṃyutta Nikāya 35

16. Nandik­kha­ya­vagga

164. Koṭ­ṭhi­ka­anatta­sutta

Ekamantaṃ … pe … vihareyyanti. “Yo kho, koṭṭhika, anattā tatra te chando pahātabbo. Ko ca, koṭṭhika, anattā? Cakkhu kho, koṭṭhika, anattā; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Rūpā anattā; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Cakkhuviññāṇaṃ anattā; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Cak­khu­samphasso anattā; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Yampidaṃ cak­khu­samphas­sa­pac­cayā uppajjati vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā dukkhaṃ vā aduk­kha­ma­su­khaṃ vā tampi anattā; tatra te chando pahātabbo … pe … jivhā anattā; tatra te chando pahātabbo … pe … mano anattā; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Dhammā anattā; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Manoviññāṇaṃ … manosamphasso … yampidaṃ mano­samphas­sa­pac­cayā uppajjati vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā dukkhaṃ vā aduk­kha­ma­su­khaṃ vā tampi anattā; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Yo kho, koṭṭhika, anattā, tatra te chando pahātabbo”ti.

Navamaṃ.
