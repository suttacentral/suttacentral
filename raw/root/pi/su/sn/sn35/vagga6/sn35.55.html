Saṃyutta Nikāya 35

6. Avijjāvagga

55. Saṃ­yoja­na­samug­ghāta­sutta

“Kathaṃ nu kho, bhante, jānato, kathaṃ passato saṃyojanā samugghātaṃ gacchantī”ti? “Cakkhuṃ kho, bhikkhu, anattato jānato passato saṃyojanā samugghātaṃ gacchanti. Rūpe anattato … cakkhuviññāṇaṃ … cak­khu­samphas­saṃ … yampidaṃ cak­khu­samphas­sa­pac­cayā uppajjati vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā dukkhaṃ vā aduk­kha­ma­su­khaṃ vā tampi anattato jānato passato saṃyojanā samugghātaṃ gacchanti. Sotaṃ … ghānaṃ … jivhaṃ … kāyaṃ … manaṃ … dhamme … manoviññāṇaṃ … manosamphassaṃ … yampidaṃ mano­samphas­sa­pac­cayā uppajjati vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā dukkhaṃ vā aduk­kha­ma­su­khaṃ vā tampi anattato jānato passato saṃyojanā samugghātaṃ gacchanti. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhu, jānato evaṃ passato saṃyojanā samugghātaṃ gacchantī”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
