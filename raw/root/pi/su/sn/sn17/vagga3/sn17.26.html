Saṃyutta Nikāya 17

3. Tatiyavagga

26. Dutiya­samaṇab­rāhma­ṇa­sutta

Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Ye hi keci, bhikkhave, samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā lābhasakkā­ra­silokassa samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ nappajānanti … pe … pajānanti … pe … sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharantī”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
