Saṃyutta Nikāya 17

4. Catutthavagga

34. Suk­ka­dham­ma­sutta

Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko. Lābhasakkā­ra­silokena abhibhūtassa ­pariyā­diṇṇa­cittassa, bhikkhave, devadattassa sukko dhammo ­samuc­cheda­magamā. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko … pe … sikkhitabban”ti.

Catutthaṃ.
