Saṃyutta Nikāya 23

1. Paṭha­ma­māravagga

7. Sotāpannasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ rādhaṃ bhagavā etadavoca: “pañcime, rādha, upādā­nak­khan­dhā. Katame pañca? Rūpupā­dā­nak­khan­dho … pe … viñ­ñāṇupā­dā­nak­khan­dho. Yato kho, rādha, ariyasāvako imesaṃ pañcannaṃ upādā­nak­khan­dhā­naṃ samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti—ayaṃ vuccati, rādha, ariyasāvako sotāpanno avini­pāta­dhammo niyato sam­bodhi­parā­yano”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
