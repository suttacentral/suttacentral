Therīapadāna

Sumedhāvagga

3. Maṇḍa­pa­dāyikā­therī­apadāna

“Koṇāgama­na­buddhassa,
maṇḍapo kārito mayā;
Dhuvaṃ ticīvaraṃdāsiṃ,
buddhassa lokabandhuno.

Yaṃ yaṃ janapadaṃ yāmi,
nigame rājadhāniyo;
Sabbattha pūjitā homi,
puñña­kammas­sidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavā.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ maṇḍapadāyikā bhikkhunī imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Maṇḍa­pa­dāyikā­theri­yāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
