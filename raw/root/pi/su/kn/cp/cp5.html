Cariyāpiṭaka

Akittivagga

Dānapāramī 5

Mahā­go­vinda­cariya

“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
satta­rāja­puro­hito;
Pūjito naradevehi,
mahā­go­vindab­rāhmaṇo.

Tadāhaṃ sattarajjesu,
Yaṃ me āsi upāyanaṃ;
Tena demi mahādānaṃ,
Akkhobbhaṃ sāgarūpamaṃ.

Na me dessaṃ dhanaṃ dhaññaṃ,
Napi natthi nicayo mayi;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
Tasmā demi varaṃ dhanan”ti.

Mahā­go­vinda­cariyaṃ pañcamaṃ.
