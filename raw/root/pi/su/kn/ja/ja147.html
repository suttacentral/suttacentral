Kakaṇṭakavagga

7. Puppha­rattajā­taka

“Nayidaṃ dukkhaṃ aduṃ dukkhaṃ,
Yaṃ maṃ tudati vāyaso;
Yaṃ sāmā puppharattena,
Kattikaṃ nānubhossatī”ti.

Puppha­rattajā­takaṃ sattamaṃ.
