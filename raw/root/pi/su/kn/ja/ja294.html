Jātaka

Tikanipāta

Kumbhavagga

4. Jambu­khā­daka­jātaka

“Koyaṃ bindussaro vaggu,
­saravan­tā­namuttamo;
Accuto jambusākhāya,
moracchāpova kūjati”.

“Kulaputtova jānāti,
kulaputtaṃ pasaṃsituṃ;
Byag­ghac­chāpa­sarī­vaṇṇa,
bhuñja samma dadāmi te”.

“Cirassaṃ vata passāmi,
musāvādī samāgate;
Vantādaṃ kuṇapādañca,
aññamaññaṃ pasaṃsake”ti.

Jambu­khā­daka­jātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
