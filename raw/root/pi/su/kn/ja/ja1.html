Apaṇṇakavagga

1. Apaṇṇakajātaka

Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammā­sambud­dhassa.

“Apaṇṇakaṃ ṭhānameke,
Dutiyaṃ āhu takkikā;
Etadaññāya medhāvī,
Taṃ gaṇhe yadapaṇṇakan”ti.

Apaṇṇa­ka­jātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
