Asam­padā­na­vagga

5. Candābhajātaka

“Candābhaṃ sūriyābhañca,
yodha paññāya gādha”ti.
Avitakkena jhānena,
hoti ābhas­sarūpa­goti.

Candā­bha­jātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
