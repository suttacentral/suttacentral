Ekakanipāta

Kakaṇṭakavagga

1. Godhajātaka

“Na pāpajana­saṃ­sevī,
accan­ta­su­kha­medhati;
Godhākulaṃ kakaṇṭāva,
kaliṃ pāpeti attanan”ti.

Godhajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
