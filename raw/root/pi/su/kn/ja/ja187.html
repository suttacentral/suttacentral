Asadisavagga

7. Catu­maṭṭha­jātaka

“Ucce viṭabhimāruyha,
mantayavho rahogatā;
Nīce oruyha mantavho,
migarājāpi sossati.

Yaṃ suvaṇṇo suvaṇṇena,
devo devena mantaye;
Kiṃ tettha catumaṭṭhassa,
bilaṃ pavisa jambukā”ti.

­Catumaṭ­ṭha­jātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
