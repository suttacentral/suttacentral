Dukanipāta

Nataṃ­daḷha­vagga

4. Vīrakajātaka

“Api vīraka passesi,
sakuṇaṃ mañjubhāṇakaṃ;
Mayūra­gīva­saṅkā­saṃ,
patiṃ mayhaṃ saviṭṭhakaṃ”.

“Uda­katha­la­carassa pakkhino,
Niccaṃ āmaka­maccha­bhojino;
Tassānukaraṃ saviṭṭhako,
Sevāle paliguṇṭhito mato”ti.

Vīrakajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
