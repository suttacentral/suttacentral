Apaṇṇakavagga

6. Devadham­ma­jātaka

“Hiri­ot­tap­pa­sam­pannā,
suk­ka­dham­ma­sa­māhitā;
Santo sappurisā loke,
devadhammāti vuccare”ti.

Devadham­ma­jātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
