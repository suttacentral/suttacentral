Sīlavagga

6. Tipal­lattha­mi­gajā­taka

“Migaṃ tipal­lattha­ma­ne­kamāyaṃ,
Aṭṭhakkhuraṃ aḍḍha­rattā­papā­yiṃ;
Ekena sotena chamāssasanto,
Chahi kalāhitibhoti bhāgineyyo”ti.

Tipal­lattha­mi­ga­jātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
