Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Āsīsavagga

10. Saṅ­kha­dhama­jātaka

“Dhame dhame nātidhame,
atidhantañhi pāpakaṃ;
Dhan­te­nādhi­gatā bhogā,
te tāto vidhamī dhaman”ti.

Saṅ­kha­dhama­jātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Āsīsavaggo chaṭṭho.

Tassuddānaṃ

Yathā icchiṃ tathāhudakā thalā,
Sura sāduphalo ca alīnamano;
Sam­pahaṭ­ṭha­mano caturo ca tayo,
Sataladdhaka bhogadhanena dasāti.
