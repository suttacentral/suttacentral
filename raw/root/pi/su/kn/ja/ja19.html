Sīlavagga

9. Āyāci­ta­bhattajā­taka

“Sace mucce pecca mucce,
Muccamāno hi bajjhati;
Na hevaṃ dhīrā muccanti,
Mutti bālassa bandhanan”ti.

Āyāci­ta­bhatta­jātakaṃ navamaṃ.
