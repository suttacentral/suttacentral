Kuruṅgavagga

1. ­Kuruṅ­ga­mi­gajā­taka

“Ñātametaṃ kuruṅgassa,
yaṃ tvaṃ sepaṇṇi siyyasi;
Aññaṃ sepaṇṇi gacchāmi,
na me te ruccate phalan”ti.

­Kuruṅ­ga­miga­jātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
