Parosatavagga

4. Mitta­vinda­ka­jātaka

“Catubbhi aṭṭhajjhagamā,
aṭṭhāhipi ca soḷasa;
Soḷasāhi ca bāttiṃsa,
atricchaṃ cakkamāsado;
Icchāhatassa posassa,
cakkaṃ bhamati matthake”ti.

Mitta­vinda­ka­jātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
