Upāhanavagga

8. Ekapadajātaka

“Iṅgha ekapadaṃ tāta,
ane­kattha­padas­sitaṃ;
Kiñci saṅgāhikaṃ brūsi,
yenatthe sādhayemase”.

“­Dak­kheyye­kapadaṃ tāta,
ane­kattha­padas­sitaṃ;
Tañca sīlena saññuttaṃ,
khantiyā upapāditaṃ;
Alaṃ mitte sukhāpetuṃ,
amittānaṃ dukhāya cā”ti.

Ekapadajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
