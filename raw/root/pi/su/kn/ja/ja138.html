Asam­padā­na­vagga

8. Godhajātaka

“Kiṃ te jaṭāhi dummedha,
kiṃ te ajinasāṭiyā;
Abbhantaraṃ te gahanaṃ,
bāhiraṃ parimajjasī”ti.

Godhajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
