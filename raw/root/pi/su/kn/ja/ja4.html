Apaṇṇakavagga

4. Cūḷa­seṭṭhi­jātaka

“Appakenapi medhāvī,
pābhatena vicakkhaṇo;
Samuṭṭhāpeti attānaṃ,
aṇuṃ aggiṃva sandhaman”ti.

Cūḷa­seṭṭhi­jātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
