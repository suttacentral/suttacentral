Ekakanipāta

Itthivagga

3. Takka­paṇ­ḍita­jātaka

“Kodhanā akataññū ca,
pisuṇā mittabhedikā;
Brahmacariyaṃ cara bhikkhu,
so sukhaṃ na vihāhasīti.

Takka­paṇ­ḍita­jātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
