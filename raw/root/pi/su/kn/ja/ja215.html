Bīraṇa­tham­bha­vagga

5. Kacchapajātaka

“Avadhī vata attānaṃ,
kacchapo byāharaṃ giraṃ;
Suggahītasmiṃ kaṭṭhasmiṃ,
vācāya sakiyāvadhi.

Etampi disvā nara­vīriya­seṭṭha,
Vācaṃ pamuñce kusalaṃ nātivelaṃ;
Passasi bahubhāṇena,
Kacchapaṃ byasanaṃ gatan”ti.

Kaccha­pa­jātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
