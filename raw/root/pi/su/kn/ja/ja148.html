Kakaṇṭakavagga

8. Siṅgālajātaka

“Nāhaṃ punaṃ na ca punaṃ,
na cāpi apunappunaṃ;
Hatthibondiṃ pavekkhāmi,
tathā hi bhayatajjito”ti.

Siṅgālajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
