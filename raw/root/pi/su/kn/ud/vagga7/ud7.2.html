Udāna 7.2

Dutiya­la­kuṇḍa­ka­bhaddi­yasutta

Evaṃ me sutaṃ—​   ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anātha­piṇḍi­kassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā sāriputto āyasmantaṃ lakuṇḍa­ka­bhaddi­yaṃ sekhaṃ maññamāno bhiyyoso mattāya aneka­pariyā­yena dhammiyā kathāya sandasseti samādapeti samuttejeti sampahaṃseti.

Addasā kho bhagavā āyasmantaṃ sāriputtaṃ āyasmantaṃ lakuṇḍa­ka­bhaddi­yaṃ sekhaṃ maññamānaṃ bhiyyoso mattāya aneka­pariyā­yena dhammiyā kathāya sandassentaṃ samādapentaṃ samuttejentaṃ sampahaṃsentaṃ.

Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi: 

“Acchecchi vaṭṭaṃ byagā nirāsaṃ,
Visukkhā saritā na sandati;
Chinnaṃ vaṭṭaṃ na vattati,
Esevanto dukkhassā”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
