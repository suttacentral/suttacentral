Ekakanipāta

9. Bhadrā­therī­gāthā

“Saddhāya pabbajitvāna,
bhadre bhadraratā bhava;
Bhāvehi kusale dhamme,
yogak­khema­ma­nutta­ran”ti.

… Bhadrā therī ….
