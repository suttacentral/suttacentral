Therāpadāna

Nāga­samāla­vagga

8. Pāṭali­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Kañ­canag­ghiya­saṅkā­saṃ,
Bāt­tiṃ­sa­vara­lak­kha­ṇaṃ.

Seṭṭhiputto tadā āsiṃ,
sukhumālo sukhedhito;
Ucchaṅge pāṭalipupphaṃ,
katvāna abhisaṃhariṃ.

Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
pupphehi abhipūjayiṃ;
Tissaṃ lokaviduṃ nāthaṃ,
naradevaṃ namassahaṃ.

Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
puppha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Ito ­tesaṭ­ṭhi­kappamhi,
abhi­samma­ta­nāmako;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pāṭalipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Pāṭali­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
