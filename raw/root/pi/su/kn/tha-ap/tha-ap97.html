Therāpadāna

Sudhāvagga

5. Gandha­māli­yat­thera­apadāna

“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
gandhathūpaṃ akāsahaṃ;
Sumanehi paṭicchannaṃ,
bud­dhānuc­chavi­kaṃ kataṃ.

Kañ­canag­ghiya­saṅkā­saṃ,
buddhaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Indīvaraṃva jalitaṃ,
ādittaṃva hutāsanaṃ.

Byag­ghūsa­bhaṃva pavaraṃ,
abhijātaṃva kesariṃ;
Nisinnaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
­bhik­khu­saṃgha­pu­rak­kha­taṃ.

Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho;
Catunnavutito kappe,
gandhamālaṃ yato adaṃ.

Buddhe katassa kārassa,
phalenāhaṃ visesato;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Cattārīsamhi ekūne,
kappe āsiṃsu soḷasa;
Deva­gan­dhasanāmā te,
rājāno cakkavattino.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Gandha­māli­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ pañcamaṃ.
