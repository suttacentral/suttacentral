Therāpadāna

Nāga­samāla­vagga

9. Ṭhi­tañjali­yat­thera­apadāna

“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
Araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Tattha addasaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Bāt­tiṃ­sa­vara­lak­kha­ṇaṃ.

Tatthāhaṃ añjaliṃ katvā,
pakkāmiṃ pācināmukho;
Avidūre nisinnassa,
niyake paṇṇasanthare.

Tato me asanīpāto,
matthake nipatī tadā;
Sohaṃ maraṇakālamhi,
akāsiṃ punarañjaliṃ.

Dvenavute ito kappe,
añjaliṃ akariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
añjalissa idaṃ phalaṃ.

Catu­paṇṇā­sa­kappamhi,
miga­ketu­sanā­mako;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ṭhitañjaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Ṭhi­tañjali­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ navamaṃ.
