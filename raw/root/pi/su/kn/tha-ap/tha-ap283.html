Therāpadāna

­Paṇṇadā­yaka­vagga

1. ­Paṇṇadā­yakat­thera­apadāna

“Paṇṇasāle nisinnomhi,
paṇṇa­bhoja­na­bhojano;
Upaviṭṭhañca maṃ santaṃ,
upāgacchi mahāisi.

Siddhattho lokapajjoto,
sabba­loka­ti­kicchako;
Tassa paṇṇaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
nisinnaṃ paṇṇasanthare.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ paṇṇamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
­paṇṇadā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paṇṇadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

­Paṇṇadā­yakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ paṭhamaṃ.
