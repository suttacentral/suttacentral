Therāpadāna

Ārak­kha­dāyaka­vagga

3. Gatasañña­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Ākāseva padaṃ natthi,
ambare anilañjase;
Siddhatthaṃ jinamaddakkhiṃ,
gacchantaṃ tidivaṅgaṇe.

Anileneritaṃ disvā,
sammā­sambud­dha­cīvaraṃ;
Vitti mamāhu tāvade,
disvāna gamanaṃ muniṃ.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bud­dhasaññā­yidaṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gatasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Gatasañña­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
