Therāpadāna

Timiravagga

9. Bodhi­siñ­ca­kat­thera­apadāna

“Vipassissa bhagavato,
mahābodhimaho ahu;
Pabbajjupagato santo,
upagacchiṃ ahaṃ tadā.

Kusu­modaka­mādāya,
bodhiyā okiriṃ ahaṃ;
Mocayissati no mutto,
nibbāpessati nibbuto.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ bodhi­ma­bhisiñ­cayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhi­siñcā­yidaṃ phalaṃ.

Tettiṃse vattamānamhi,
kappe āsuṃ janādhipā;
Udakasecanā nāma,
aṭṭhete cakkavattino.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bodhisiñcako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Bodhi­siñ­ca­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ navamaṃ.
