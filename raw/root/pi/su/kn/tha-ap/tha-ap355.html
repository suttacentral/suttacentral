Therāpadāna

Sad­dasañña­ka­vagga

3. Kiṃ­suka­pūja­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Kiṃsukaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
paggahetvāna añjaliṃ;
Buddhaṃ saritvā siddhatthaṃ,
ākāse abhipūjayiṃ.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṃsukapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Kiṃ­suka­pūja­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
