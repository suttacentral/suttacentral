Therāpadāna

Bandhu­jīvaka­vagga

3. Vīthi­sammaj­jakat­thera­apadāna

“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Pannarase yathā candaṃ,
niyyantaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

Aṭṭha­saṭṭhi­sahas­sāni,
sabbe khīṇāsavā ahuṃ;
Parivāriṃsu sambuddhaṃ,
dvipadindaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

Sammajjitvāna taṃ vīthiṃ,
niyyante lokanāyake;
Ussāpesiṃ dhajaṃ tattha,
vippasannena cetasā.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ dhajaṃ abhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhaja­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Ito catutthake kappe,
rājāhosiṃ mahabbalo;
Sabbākārena sampanno,
sudhajo iti vissuto.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vīthi­sammaj­jako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Vīthi­sammaj­jakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
