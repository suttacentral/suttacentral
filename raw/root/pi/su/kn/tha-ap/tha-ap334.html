Therāpadāna

­Gan­dho­da­ka­vagga

2. Udaka­pūja­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ anilañjase;
Ghatāsanaṃva jalitaṃ,
Ādittaṃva hutāsanaṃ.

Pāṇinā udakaṃ gayha,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ;
Sampaṭicchi mahāvīro,
buddho kāruṇiko isi.

Antalikkhe ṭhito satthā,
padu­muttara­nā­mako;
Mama saṅ­kappa­maññāya,
imaṃ gātha­mabhāsa­tha.

‘Iminā dakadānena,
pītiuppādanena ca;
Kappa­sata­sahas­sampi,
duggatiṃ nupapajjati’.

Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

Sahas­sa­rā­janā­mena,
tayo ca cakkavattino;
Pañca­saṭṭhi­kappa­sate,
cāturantā janādhipā.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā udakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Udaka­pūja­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
