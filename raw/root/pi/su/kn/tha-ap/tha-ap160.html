Therāpadāna

Bandhu­jīvaka­vagga

8. Nāga­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Suvaccho nāma nāmena,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū;
Purakkhato sasissehi,
vasate pabbatantare.

Padumuttaro nāma jino,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Mamuddha­ri­tukāmo so,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

Vehāsamhi caṅkamati,
dhūpāyati jalate tathā;
Hāsaṃ mamaṃ viditvāna,
pakkāmi pācināmukho.

Tañca acchariyaṃ disvā,
abbhutaṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Nāgapupphaṃ gahetvāna,
gatamaggamhi okiriṃ.

Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ pupphaṃ okiriṃ ahaṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

Ekattiṃse kappasate,
rājā āsi mahāraho;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nāgapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Nāga­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
