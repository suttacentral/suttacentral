Therāpadāna

Ārak­kha­dāyaka­vagga

7. Sad­dasañña­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Sudassano mahāvīro,
deseti amataṃ padaṃ;
Parivuto sāvakehi,
vasati gharamuttame.

Tāya vācāya madhurāya,
saṅgaṇhāti mahājanaṃ;
Ghoso ca vipulo āsi,
āsīso devamānuse.

Nigghosasaddaṃ sutvāna,
siddhatthassa mahesino;
Sadde cittaṃ pasādetvā,
avandiṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bud­dhasaññā­yidaṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Sad­dasañña­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ sattamaṃ.
