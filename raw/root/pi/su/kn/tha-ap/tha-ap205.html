Therāpadāna

­Kaṇikā­ra­pupphi­yavagga

3 Kiṅ­ka­ṇi­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Kañ­canag­ghi­yasaṅkāso,
sabbaññū lokanāyako;
Odakaṃ dahamoggayha,
sināyi lokanāyako.

Paggayha kiṅkaṇiṃ pupphaṃ,
Vipas­sis­sā­bhi­ro­payiṃ;
Udaggacitto sumano,
Dvipadindassa tādino.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Satta­vīsati­kappamhi,
rājā bhīmaratho ahu;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṅ­ka­ṇi­pupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Kiṅ­ka­ṇi­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
