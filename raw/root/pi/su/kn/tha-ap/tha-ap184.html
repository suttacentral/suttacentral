Therāpadāna

Kuṭaja­pupphi­yavagga

2. Bandhu­jīvakat­thera­apadāna

“Siddhattho nāma sambuddho,
sayambhū sabbhi vaṇṇito;
Samādhiṃ so samāpanno,
nisīdi pabbatantare.

Jātassare gavesanto,
dakajaṃ pupphamuttamaṃ;
Bandhu­jīvaka­pupphāni,
addasaṃ samanantaraṃ.

Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
upāgacchiṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
siddhat­thas­sā­bhi­ro­payiṃ.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Ito cātuddase kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Samuddakappo nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bandhujīvako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Bandhu­jīvakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
