Therāpadāna

Udakāsanavagga

7. Piyāla­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Piyālaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
gatamagge khipiṃ ahaṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā piyālapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Piyāla­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ sattamaṃ.
