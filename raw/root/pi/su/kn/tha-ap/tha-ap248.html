Therāpadāna

Tuvaradā­yaka­vagga

6. Patta­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Paramena damathena,
siddhatthassa mahesino;
Pattadānaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
ujubhūtassa tādino.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
patta­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pattadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Patta­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
