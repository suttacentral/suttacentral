Therāpadāna

Udakāsanavagga

3. Sāla­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Aruṇavatiyā nagare,
ahosiṃ pūpiko tadā;
Mama dvārena gacchantaṃ,
sikhinaṃ addasaṃ jinaṃ.

Buddhassa pattaṃ paggayha,
sālapupphaṃ adāsahaṃ;
Sammaggatassa buddhassa,
vippasannena cetasā.

Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
Yaṃ puppha­mabhi­dāsa­haṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
Sāla­pupphas­sidaṃ phalaṃ.

Ito ­cudda­sa­kappamhi,
ahosiṃ amitañjalo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sālapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Sāla­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
