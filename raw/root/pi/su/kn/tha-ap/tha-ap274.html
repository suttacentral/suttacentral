Therāpadāna

Suvaṇ­ṇa­bibboha­navagga

2 Tila­muṭṭhi­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Mama saṅ­kappa­maññāya,
satthā lokagganāyako;
Manomayena kāyena,
iddhiyā upasaṅkami.

Satthāraṃ upasaṅkantaṃ,
vanditvā purisuttamaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
tila­muṭṭhi­madāsa­haṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tila­muṭṭhi­yidaṃ phalaṃ.

Ito soḷasakappamhi,
tantiso nāma khattiyo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tila­muṭṭhi­dāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Tila­muṭṭhi­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
