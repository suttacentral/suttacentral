Therāpadāna

Nāga­samāla­vagga

3 Bud­dhasañña­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Dumagge paṃsukūlikaṃ,
laggaṃ disvāna satthuno;
Tato tamañjaliṃ katvā,
paṃsukūlaṃ avandahaṃ.

Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bud­dhasaññā­yidaṃ phalaṃ.

Ito catutthake kappe,
dumasārosi khattiyo;
Cāturanto vijitāvī,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā buddhasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Bud­dhasañña­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
