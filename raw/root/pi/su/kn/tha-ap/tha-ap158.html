Therāpadāna

Bandhu­jīvaka­vagga

6. Kadam­ba­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Hima­van­tassā­vidūre,
kukkuṭo nāma pabbato;
Tamhi pabbatapādamhi,
satta buddhā vasanti te.

Kadambaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
dīparājaṃva uggataṃ;
Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
satta buddhe samokiriṃ.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Dvenavute ito kappe,
sattāsuṃ pupphanāmakā;
Satta­ratana­sam­pannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kadam­ba­pupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Kadam­ba­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
