Therāpadāna

Timiravagga

7. Phala­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Pabbate himavantamhi,
kharājinadharo ahaṃ;
Phussaṃ jinavaraṃ disvā,
phalahattho phalaṃ adaṃ.

Yamahaṃ phalamadāsiṃ,
vippasannena cetasā;
Bhave nib­batta­mānamhi,
phalaṃ nibbattate mama.

Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Phala­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ sattamaṃ.
