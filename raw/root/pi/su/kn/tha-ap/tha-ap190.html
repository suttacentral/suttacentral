Therāpadāna

Kuṭaja­pupphi­yavagga

8. Tikaṇ­ṇi­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Devabhūto ahaṃ santo,
accharāhi purakkhato;
Pubbakammaṃ saritvāna,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ anussariṃ.

Tikaṇṇipupphaṃ paggayha,
sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Buddhamhi abhiropesiṃ,
vipassimhi narāsabhe.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Tesattatimhito kappe,
caturāsuṃ ramuttamā;
Satta­ratana­sam­pannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tikaṇ­ṇi­pupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Tikaṇ­ṇi­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
