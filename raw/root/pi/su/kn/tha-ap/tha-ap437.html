Therāpadāna

Ekavihārivagga

5. Ucchu­khaṇ­ḍikat­thera­apadāna

“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
dvārapālo ahosahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sabbadhammāna pāraguṃ.

Ucchu­khaṇ­ḍika­mādāya,
buddha­seṭṭhas­sadāsa­haṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
vipassissa mahesino.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ ucchumadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ucchu­khaṇ­ḍas­sidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ucchukhaṇḍiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Ucchu­khaṇ­ḍikat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ pañcamaṃ.
