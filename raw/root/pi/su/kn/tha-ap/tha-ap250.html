Therāpadāna

Tuvaradā­yaka­vagga

8 Satta­li­puppha­pūja­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Satta satta­li­pupphāni,
sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
vessabhumhi naruttame.

Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā satta­li­puppha­pūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Satta­li­puppha­pūja­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
