Therāpadāna

Bodhi­vanda­na­vagga

7. Citaka­pūja­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Parinibbute bhagavati,
jala­juttama­nāmake;
Āropitamhi citake,
sāla­puppha­mapū­jayiṃ.

Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Citaka­pūja­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ sattamaṃ.
