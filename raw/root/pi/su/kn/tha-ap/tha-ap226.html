Therāpadāna

Ālamba­ṇa­dāyaka­vagga

4. Ārak­kha­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
vedi kārāpitā mayā;
Ārakkho ca mayā dinno,
sugatassa mahesino.

Tena kammavisesena,
na passiṃ bhayabheravaṃ;
Kuhiñci upapannassa,
tāso mayhaṃ na vijjati.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ vediṃ kārayiṃ pure;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vedikāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

Ito chaṭṭhamhi kappamhi,
apas­se­nasa­nā­mako;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ārakkhadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Ārak­kha­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ catutthaṃ.
