Therāpadāna

Ava­ṭaphala­vagga

2. Labuja­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
āsiṃ ārāmiko tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
gacchantaṃ anilañjase.

Labujassa phalaṃ gayha,
buddha­seṭṭhas­sadāsa­haṃ;
Ākāse ṭhitako santo,
paṭigaṇhi mahāyaso.

Vitti­sañjana­naṃ mayhaṃ,
diṭṭha­dhamma­su­khā­vahaṃ;
Phalaṃ buddhassa datvāna,
vippasannena cetasā.

Adhigacchiṃ tadā pītiṃ,
vipulañca sukhuttamaṃ;
Uppajjateva ratanaṃ,
nibbattassa tahiṃ tahiṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā labujadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Labuja­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
