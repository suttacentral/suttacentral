Therāpadāna

Jagati­dāyaka­vagga

2. Mora­hatthi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Morahatthaṃ gahetvāna,
upesiṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
mora­hattha­ma­dā­sahaṃ.

Iminā morahatthena,
cetanā­paṇi­dhīhi ca;
Nibbāyiṃsu tayo aggī,
labhāmi vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

Aho buddhā aho dhammā,
aho no satthusampadā;
Datvānahaṃ morahatthaṃ,
labhāmi vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

Tiyaggī nibbutā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
mora­hatthas­sidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā morahatthiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Mora­hatthi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
