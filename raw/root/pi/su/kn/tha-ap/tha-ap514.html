Therāpadāna

Phala­dāyaka­vagga

2 Kapit­tha­phala­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
Kapitthaṃ adadiṃ phalaṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kapit­tha­pha­ladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Kapit­tha­phala­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
