Therāpadāna

Bodhi­vanda­na­vagga

6. Gandha­muṭṭhi­yat­thera­apadāna

* “Citake karīyamāne,
nānāgandhe samāhate;
Pasannacitto sumano,
gandha­muṭṭhi­mapū­jayiṃ.

Satasahassito kappe,
citakaṃ yaṃ apūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhamuṭṭhiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Gandha­muṭṭhi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
