Therāpadāna

Tamāla­pupphi­yavagga

2 Tiṇasan­thāra­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Yaṃ dāyavāsiko isi,
tiṇaṃ lāyati satthuno;
Sabbe padak­khi­ṇā­vaṭṭā,
pathabyā nipatiṃsu te.

Tamahaṃ tiṇamādāya,
santhariṃ dharaṇuttame;
Tīṇeva tālapattāni,
āharitvānahaṃ tadā.

Tiṇena chadanaṃ katvā,
siddhatthassa adāsahaṃ;
Sattāhaṃ dhārayuṃ tassa,
deva­mānu­sa­satthuno.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ tiṇaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tiṇadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

Pañca­saṭṭhim­hito kappe,
cattārosuṃ mahaddhanā;
Satta­ratana­sam­pannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇasanthārako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Tiṇasan­thāra­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
