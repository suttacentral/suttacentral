Therāpadāna

Ārak­kha­dāyaka­vagga

5. Pupphā­sanadā­ya­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Avidūrena gacchantaṃ,
Siddhatthaṃ aparājitaṃ.

Tassa pac­cugga­mit­vāna,
pavesetvāna assamaṃ;
Pupphāsanaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
vippasannena cetasā.

Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
vedajāto tadā ahaṃ;
Buddhe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
taṃ kammaṃ pariṇāmayiṃ.

Yaṃ me atthi kataṃ puññaṃ,
sayam­bhum­haparā­jite;
Sabbena tena kusalena,
vimalo homi sāsane.

Catunnavutito kappe,
pup­phāsana­madaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pup­phāsanas­sidaṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pupphā­sanadā­yako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Pupphā­sanadā­ya­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ pañcamaṃ.
