Therāpadāna

Suvaṇ­ṇa­bibboha­navagga

5 Ekañjali­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Udumbare vasantassa,
niyate paṇṇasanthare;
Vutthokāso mayā dinno,
samaṇassa mahesino.

Tissassa dvipadindassa,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
santhariṃ puppha­san­tha­raṃ.

Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kariṃ puppha­san­tha­raṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
santharassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

Ito ­cudda­sa­kappamhi,
ahosiṃ manujādhipo;
Ekaañjaliko nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekañjaliko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Ekañjali­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ pañcamaṃ.
