Therāpadāna

Mandā­ra­va­pupphi­yavagga

10 Saḷala­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Canda­bhāgā­nadī­tīre,
ahosiṃ kinnaro tadā;
Tatthaddasaṃ devadevaṃ,
caṅkamantaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

Ocinitvāna saḷalaṃ,
pupphaṃ bud­dhassadā­sahaṃ;
Upasiṅghi mahāvīro,
saḷalaṃ devagandhikaṃ.

Paṭiggahetvā sambuddho,
vipassī lokanāyako;
Upasiṅghi mahāvīro,
pekkhamānassa me sato.

Pasannacitto sumano,
vanditvā dvipaduttamaṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
puna pabbatamāruhiṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (3213)

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saḷalapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Saḷala­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dasamaṃ.

Mandā­ra­va­pupphi­yavaggo sattatiṃsatimo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Mandāravañca kakkāru,
bhisa­kesara­pupphiyo;
Aṅkolako kadambī ca,
uddālī ekacampako;
Timiraṃ saḷalañceva,
gāthā tālīsameva ca.
