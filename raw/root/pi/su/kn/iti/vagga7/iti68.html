Itivuttaka 68

Paṭha­ma­rāga­sutta

Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā ­vuttama­rahatāti me sutaṃ: 

“Yassa kassaci, bhikkhave, rāgo appahīno, doso appahīno, moho appahīno—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘baddho mārassa paṭimukkassa mārapāso yathā­kāma­ka­raṇīyo pāpimato’. Yassa kassaci, bhikkhave, rāgo pahīno, doso pahīno, moho pahīno—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘abaddho mārassa omukkassa mārapāso na yathā­kāma­ka­raṇīyo pāpimato’”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

“Yassa rāgo ca doso ca,
avijjā ca virājitā;
Taṃ bhāvi­tat­tañ­ñata­raṃ,
brahmabhūtaṃ tathāgataṃ;
Buddhaṃ verabhayātītaṃ,
āhu sabbap­pahāyi­nan”ti.

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Navamaṃ.
