Ekakanipāta

Navamavagga

1.85. Sunā­gat­thera­gāthā

“Citta­nimittassa kovido,
Pavivekarasaṃ vijāniya;
Jhāyaṃ nipako patissato,
Adhigaccheyya sukhaṃ nirāmisan”ti.

… Sunāgo thero ….
