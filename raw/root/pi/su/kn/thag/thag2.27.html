Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Tatiyavagga

2.27. Nanda­kat­thera­gāthā

“Yathāpi bhaddo ājañño,
khalitvā patitiṭṭhati;
Bhiyyo laddhāna saṃvegaṃ,
adīno vahate dhuraṃ.

Evaṃ das­sa­nasam­pannaṃ,
sammā­sambud­dha­sāvakaṃ;
Ājānīyaṃ maṃ dhāretha,
puttaṃ buddhassa orasan”ti.

… Nandako thero ….
