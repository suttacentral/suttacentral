Ekādasamavagga

1.108. Dhamma­sa­vapi­tut­thera­gāthā

“Sa vīsa­vassasa­tiko,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

… Dhammasavapitu thero ….
