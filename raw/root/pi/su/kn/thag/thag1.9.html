Paṭhamavagga

1.9 Pilinda­vacchat­thera­gāthā

“Svāgataṃ na durāgataṃ,
Nayidaṃ dumantitaṃ mama;
Saṃvibhattesu dhammesu,
Yaṃ seṭṭhaṃ tadupāgamin”ti.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pilindavaccho thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
