Ekakanipāta

Aṭṭhamavagga

1.78. Meṇḍa­sirat­thera­gāthā

“Aneka­jāti­saṃsā­raṃ,
sandhāvissaṃ anibbisaṃ;
Tassa me dukkhajātassa,
dukkhakkhandho aparaddho”ti.

… Meṇḍasiro thero ….
