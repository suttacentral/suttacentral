Ekakanipāta

Tatiyavagga

1.25. Nandi­yat­thera­gāthā

“Obhāsajātaṃ phalagaṃ,
cittaṃ yassa abhiṇhaso;
Tādisaṃ bhikkhumāsajja,
kaṇha dukkhaṃ nigacchasī”ti.

… Nandiyo thero ….
