Ekakanipāta

Dvādasamavagga

1.111. Jentat­thera­gāthā

“Duppabbajjaṃ ve duradhivāsā gehā,
Dhammo gambhīro duradhigamā bhogā;
Kicchā vutti no itarītareneva,
Yuttaṃ cintetuṃ satata­ma­nicca­tan”ti.

… Jento thero ….
