Pañcamavagga

1.41. Siri­vaḍḍhat­thera­gāthā

“Vivara­manupa­tanti vijjutā,
Vebhārassa ca paṇḍavassa ca;
Nagavivaragato ca jhāyati,
Putto appaṭimassa tādino”ti.

… Sirivaḍḍho thero ….
