Aṭṭhamavagga

1.73. Māṇavat­thera­gāthā

“Jiṇṇañca disvā dukhitañca byādhitaṃ,
Matañca disvā gata­māyu­saṅ­kha­yaṃ;
Tato ahaṃ nikkhamitūna pabbajiṃ,
Pahāya kāmāni manoramānī”ti.

… Māṇavo thero ….
