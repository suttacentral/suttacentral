Paṭhamavagga

6.2. Tekiccha­kārit­thera­gāthā

“Atihitā vīhi,
khalagatā sālī;
Na ca labhe piṇḍaṃ,
kathamahaṃ kassaṃ”.

“Buddha­mappa­meyyaṃ anussara pasanno,
Pītiyā phuṭasarīro hohisi satatamudaggo.

Dhamma­mappa­meyyaṃ anussara pasanno,
Pītiyā phuṭasarīro hohisi satatamudaggo.

Saṃ­gha­mappa­meyyaṃ anussara pasanno,
Pītiyā phuṭasarīro hohisi satatamudaggo”.

“Abbhokāse viharasi,
Sītā hemantikā imā ratyo;
Mā sītena pareto vihaññittho,
Pavisa tvaṃ vihāraṃ phusitaggaḷaṃ”.

“Phusissaṃ catasso appamaññāyo,
Tāhi ca sukhito viharissaṃ;
Nāhaṃ sītena vihaññissaṃ,
Aniñjito viharanto”ti.

… Tekicchakārī thero ….
