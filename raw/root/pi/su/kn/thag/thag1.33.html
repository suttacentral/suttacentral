Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Catutthavagga

1.33. Sopākat­thera­gāthā

“Yathāpi ekaputtasmiṃ,
piyasmiṃ kusalī siyā;
Evaṃ sabbesu pāṇesu,
sabbattha kusalo siyā”ti.

… Sopāko thero ….
