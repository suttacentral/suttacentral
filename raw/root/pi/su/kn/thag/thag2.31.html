Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Catutthavagga

2.31. Miga­sirat­thera­gāthā

“Yato ahaṃ pabbajito,
sammā­sambud­dha­sāsane;
Vimuccamāno uggacchiṃ,
kāmadhātuṃ upaccagaṃ.

Brahmuno pekkhamānassa,
tato cittaṃ vimucci me;
Akuppā me vimuttīti,
sabba­saṃ­yoja­nak­khayā”ti.

… Migasiro thero ….
