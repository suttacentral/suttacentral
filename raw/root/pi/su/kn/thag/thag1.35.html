Catutthavagga

1.35. Sāmañ­ña­kānit­thera­gāthā

“Sukhaṃ sukhattho labhate tadācaraṃ,
Kittiñca pappoti yasassa vaḍḍhati;
Yo ariya­maṭṭhaṅ­gika­mañjasaṃ ujuṃ,
Bhāveti maggaṃ amatassa pattiyā”ti.

… Sāmañ­ña­kānit­thero ….
