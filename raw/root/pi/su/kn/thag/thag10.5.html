Theragāthā

Dasakanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

10.5. Kappat­thera­gāthā

“­Nānā­kula­malasam­puṇṇo,
mahā­ukkāra­sam­bhavo;
Candanikaṃva paripakkaṃ,
mahāgaṇḍo mahāvaṇo.

Pubba­ruhira­sam­puṇṇo,
gūthakūpena gāḷhito;
Āpopaggharaṇo kāyo,
sadā sandati pūtikaṃ.

­Saṭṭhi­kaṇ­ḍa­rasam­ban­dho,
maṃ­salepa­na­lepito;
Camma­kañ­cuka­sannad­dho,
pūtikāyo niratthako.

Aṭṭhi­saṅghā­ta­ghaṭito,
nhāru­sutta­ni­ban­dhano;
Nekesaṃ saṅgatībhāvā,
kappeti iriyāpathaṃ.

Dhuvappayāto maraṇāya,
maccurājassa santike;
Idheva chaḍḍayitvāna,
yenakāmaṅgamo naro.

Avijjāya nivuto kāyo,
catuganthena ganthito;
Oghasaṃsīdano kāyo,
anusa­ya­jāla­motthato.

Pañcanīvaraṇe yutto,
vitakkena samappito;
Taṇhā­mūle­nānugato,
mohacchā­dana­chā­dito.

Evāyaṃ vattate kāyo,
kammayantena yantito;
Sampatti ca vipatyantā,
nānābhāvo vipajjati.

Yemaṃ kāyaṃ mamāyanti,
andhabālā puthujjanā;
Vaḍḍhenti kaṭasiṃ ghoraṃ,
ādiyanti punabbhavaṃ.

Yemaṃ kāyaṃ vivajjenti,
Gūthalittaṃva pannagaṃ;
Bhavamūlaṃ vamitvāna,
Pari­nibbis­santi­nāsavā”ti.

… Kappo thero ….
