Ekakanipāta

Navamavagga

1.82. ­Kassa­pat­thera­gāthā

“Yena yena subhikkhāni,
sivāni abhayāni ca;
Tena puttaka gacchassu,
mā sokāpahato bhavā”ti.

… Kassapo thero ….
