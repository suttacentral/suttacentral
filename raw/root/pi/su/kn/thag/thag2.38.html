Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Catutthavagga

2.38. Nisa­bhat­thera­gāthā

“Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,
Piyarūpe manorame;
Saddhāya gharā nikkhamma,
Duk­khas­santa­karo bhave.

Nābhinandāmi maraṇaṃ,
nābhinandāmi jīvitaṃ;
Kālañca paṭikaṅkhāmi,
sampajāno patissato”ti.

… Nisabho thero ….
