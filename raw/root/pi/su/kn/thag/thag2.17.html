Dukanipāta

Dutiyavagga

2.17. Tissat­thera­gāthā

“Bahū sapatte labhati,
muṇḍo saṅghāṭipāruto;
Lābhī annassa pānassa,
vatthassa sayanassa ca.

Etamādīnavaṃ ñatvā,
sakkāresu mahabbhayaṃ;
Appalābho anavassuto,
sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.

… Tisso thero ….
