Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Tatiyavagga

1.30. Utti­yat­thera­gāthā

“Ābādhe me samuppanne,
sati me udapajjatha;
Ābādho me samuppanno,
kālo me nappamajjitun”ti.

… Uttiyo thero ….
Vaggo tatiyo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Nigrodho cittako thero,
gosālathero sugandho;
Nandiyo abhayo thero,
thero lomasakaṅgiyo;
Jambu­gāmika­putto ca,
hārito uttiyo isīti.
