Chaṭṭhavagga

1.56. Kuṭi­vihārit­thera­gāthā

“Ko kuṭikāyaṃ bhikkhu kuṭikāyaṃ,
Vītarāgo susa­māhi­ta­citto;
Evaṃ jānāhi āvuso,
Amoghā te kuṭikā katā”ti.

… Kuṭi­vihārit­thero ….
