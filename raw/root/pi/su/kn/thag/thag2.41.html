Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Pañcamavagga

2.41. Kumāra­kas­sapat­thera­gāthā

“Aho buddhā aho dhammā,
aho no satthu sampadā;
Yattha etādisaṃ dhammaṃ,
sāvako sacchikāhiti.

Asaṅkheyyesu kappesu,
sakkāyādhigatā ahū;
Tesamayaṃ pacchimako,
carimoyaṃ samussayo;
Jāti­maraṇa­saṃsāro,
natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti.

… Kumārakassapo thero ….
