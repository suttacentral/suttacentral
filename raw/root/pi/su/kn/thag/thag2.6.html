Dukanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

2.6 Meḷajinat­thera­gāthā

“Yadāhaṃ dhammamassosiṃ,
bhāsamānassa satthuno;
Na kaṅ­kha­mabhi­jānāmi,
sabbañ­ñū­aparā­jite.

Satthavāhe mahāvīre,
sārathīnaṃ varuttame;
Magge paṭipadāyaṃ vā,
kaṅkhā mayhaṃ na vijjatī”ti.

… Meḷajino thero ….
