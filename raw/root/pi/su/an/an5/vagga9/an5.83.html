Aṅguttara Nikāya 5

9. Theravagga

83. Kuhakasutta

“Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato thero bhikkhu sab­rahma­cārī­naṃ appiyo ca hoti amanāpo ca agaru ca abhāvanīyo ca. Katamehi pañcahi? Kuhako ca hoti, lapako ca, nemittiko ca, nippesiko ca, lābhena ca lābhaṃ nijigīsitā—imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato thero bhikkhu sab­rahma­cārī­naṃ appiyo ca hoti amanāpo ca agaru ca abhāvanīyo ca.

Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato thero bhikkhu sab­rahma­cārī­naṃ piyo ca hoti manāpo ca garu ca bhāvanīyo ca. Katamehi pañcahi? Na ca kuhako hoti, na ca lapako, na ca nemittiko, na ca nippesiko, na ca lābhena lābhaṃ nijigīsitā— imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato thero bhikkhu sab­rahma­cārī­naṃ piyo ca hoti manāpo ca garu ca bhāvanīyo cā”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
