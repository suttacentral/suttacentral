Aṅguttara Nikāya 5

29. Rāgapeyyāla

303 

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame pañca? Asubhasaññā, maraṇasaññā, ādīnavasaññā, āhāre paṭikūlasaññā, sabbaloke anabhi­rata­saññā—rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti. (1)
