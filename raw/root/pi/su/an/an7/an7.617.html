Aṅguttara Nikāya 7

11. Rāgapeyyāla

617 

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya satta dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame satta? Asubhasaññā, maraṇasaññā, āhāre paṭikūlasaññā, sabbaloke anabhi­rata­saññā, aniccasaññā, anicce dukkhasaññā, dukkhe anattasaññā—rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime satta dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
