Aṅguttara Nikāya 7

2. Anusayavagga

20. Nidda­sa­vatthu­sutta

“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, nidda­sa­vatthūni. Katamāni satta? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sikkhāsamādāne tibbacchando hoti āyatiñca sikkhāsamādāne avigatapemo, dhamma­ni­santiyā tibbacchando hoti āyatiñca dhamma­ni­santiyā avigatapemo, icchāvinaye tibbacchando hoti āyatiñca icchāvinaye avigatapemo, paṭisallāne tibbacchando hoti āyatiñca paṭisallāne avigatapemo, vīriyārambhe tibbacchando hoti āyatiñca vīriyārambhe avigatapemo, satinepakke tibbacchando hoti āyatiñca satinepakke avigatapemo, diṭṭhi­paṭi­vedhe tibbacchando hoti āyatiñca diṭṭhi­paṭi­vedhe avigatapemo. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta nidda­sa­vatthūnī”ti.

Dasamaṃ.

Anusayavaggo dutiyo.

Duve anusayā kulaṃ,
Puggalaṃ udakūpamaṃ;
Aniccaṃ dukkhaṃ anattā ca,
Nibbānaṃ niddasavatthu cāti.
