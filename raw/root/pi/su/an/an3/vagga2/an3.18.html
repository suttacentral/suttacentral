Aṅguttara Nikāya 3

2. Rathakāravagga

18. Devalokasutta

“Sace vo, bhikkhave, aññatitthiyā paribbājakā evaṃ puccheyyuṃ: ‘deva­lo­kū­papat­tiyā, āvuso, samaṇe gotame brahmacariyaṃ vussathā’ti? Nanu tumhe, bhikkhave, evaṃ puṭṭhā aṭṭīyeyyātha harāyeyyātha jiguccheyyāthā”ti? “Evaṃ, bhante”. “Iti kira tumhe, bhikkhave, dibbena āyunā aṭṭīyatha harāyatha jigucchatha, dibbena vaṇṇena dibbena sukhena dibbena yasena dibbe­nādhipa­teyyena aṭṭīyatha harāyatha jigucchatha; pageva kho pana, bhikkhave, tumhehi kāya­ducca­ritena aṭṭīyitabbaṃ harāyitabbaṃ jigucchitabbaṃ, vacī­ducca­ritena … mano­ducca­ritena aṭṭīyitabbaṃ harāyitabbaṃ jigucchitabban”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
