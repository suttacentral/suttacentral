Aṅguttara Nikāya 3

11. Sambodhavagga

108. Atittisutta

“Tiṇṇaṃ, bhikkhave, paṭisevanāya natthi titti. Katamesaṃ tiṇṇaṃ? Soppassa, bhikkhave, paṭisevanāya natthi titti. Surā­meraya­pānassa, bhikkhave, paṭisevanāya natthi titti. Methuna­dhamma­samā­pattiyā, bhikkhave, paṭisevanāya natthi titti. Imesaṃ, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṃ paṭisevanāya natthi tittī”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
