Aṅguttara Nikāya 3

11. Sambodhavagga

110. Byāpannasutta

Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho anāthapiṇḍikaṃ gahapatiṃ bhagavā etadavoca: “citte, gahapati, byāpanne kāyakammampi byāpannaṃ hoti, vacīkammampi byāpannaṃ hoti, manokammampi byāpannaṃ hoti. Tassa ­byāpan­na­kāya­kamman­tassa ­byāpan­na­vacī­kamman­tassa ­byāpan­na­manokam­man­tassa na bhaddakaṃ maraṇaṃ hoti, na bhaddikā kālakiriyā. Seyyathāpi, gahapati, kūṭāgāre ducchanne kūṭampi byāpannaṃ hoti, gopānasiyopi byāpannā honti, bhittipi byāpannā hoti; evamevaṃ kho, gahapati, citte byāpanne kāyakammampi byāpannaṃ hoti, vacīkammampi byāpannaṃ hoti, manokammampi byāpannaṃ hoti. Tassa ­byāpan­na­kāya­kamman­tassa ­byāpan­na­vacī­kamman­tassa ­byāpan­na­manokam­man­tassa na bhaddakaṃ maraṇaṃ hoti, na bhaddikā kālakiriyā.

Citte, gahapati, abyāpanne kāyakammampi abyāpannaṃ hoti, vacīkammampi abyāpannaṃ hoti, manokammampi abyāpannaṃ hoti. Tassa abyāpan­na­kāya­kamman­tassa abyāpan­na­vacī­kamman­tassa abyāpan­na­manokam­man­tassa bhaddakaṃ maraṇaṃ hoti, bhaddikā kālakiriyā. Seyyathāpi, gahapati, kūṭāgāre succhanne kūṭampi abyāpannaṃ hoti, gopānasiyopi abyāpannā honti, bhittipi abyāpannā hoti; evamevaṃ kho, gahapati, citte abyāpanne kāyakammampi abyāpannaṃ hoti, vacīkammampi abyāpannaṃ hoti, manokammampi abyāpannaṃ hoti. Tassa abyāpan­na­kāya­kamman­tassa … pe … abyāpan­na­manokam­man­tassa bhaddakaṃ maraṇaṃ hoti, bhaddikā kālakiriyā”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
