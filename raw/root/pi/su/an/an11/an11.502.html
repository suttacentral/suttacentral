Aṅguttara Nikāya 11

3. Sāmaññavagga

502–​981 

Ekādasahi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgato gopālako bhabbo gogaṇaṃ pariharituṃ phātikātuṃ. Katamehi ekādasahi? Idha, bhikkhave, gopālako rūpaññū hoti … pe ….

Evameva kho, bhikkhave, ekādasahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu bhabbo cakkhusmiṃ aniccānupassī viharituṃ … pe … paṭi­nissag­gā­nu­passī viharitunti.
