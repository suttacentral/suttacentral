Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

27. Kamma­patha­vagga

270. Samphap­palā­pa­sutta

… pe … Attanā ca samphappalāpī hoti, parañca samphappalāpe samādapeti, samphappalāpe ca samanuñño hoti, samphap­palā­passa ca vaṇṇaṃ bhāsati—imehi … pe ….

Attanā ca samphappalāpā paṭivirato hoti, parañca samphappalāpā veramaṇiyā samādapeti, samphappalāpā veramaṇiyā ca samanuñño hoti, samphappalāpā veramaṇiyā ca vaṇṇaṃ bhāsati—imehi kho, bhikkhave … pe ….

Sattamaṃ.
