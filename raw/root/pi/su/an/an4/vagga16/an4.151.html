Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

16. Indriyavagga

151. Indriyasutta

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni cattāri? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri indriyānī”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
