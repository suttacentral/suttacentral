Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

24. Kammavagga

232. Saṅkhittasutta

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, kammāni mayā sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā paveditāni. Katamāni cattāri? Atthi, bhikkhave, kammaṃ kaṇhaṃ kaṇhavipākaṃ; atthi, bhikkhave, kammaṃ sukkaṃ sukkavipākaṃ; atthi, bhikkhave, kammaṃ kaṇhasukkaṃ kaṇha­sukka­vipākaṃ; atthi, bhikkhave, kammaṃ akaṇhaasukkaṃ akaṇha­asukka­vipākaṃ kammakkhayāya saṃvattati. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri kammāni mayā sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā paveditānī”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
