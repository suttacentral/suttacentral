Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

3. Uruvelavagga

25. Brahma­cari­ya­sutta

“Nayidaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati jana­ku­hanat­thaṃ, na janala­panat­thaṃ, na lābhasakkā­ra­silokā­ni­saṃsat­thaṃ, na iti­vādap­pamok­khā­ni­saṃsat­thaṃ, na ‘iti maṃ jano jānātū’ti. Atha kho idaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati saṃvaratthaṃ pahānatthaṃ virāgatthaṃ nirodhatthanti.

Saṃvaratthaṃ pahānatthaṃ,
brahmacariyaṃ anītihaṃ;
Adesayi so bhagavā,
nibbā­nogadha­gā­minaṃ;
Esa maggo mahantehi,
anuyāto mahesibhi.

Ye ca taṃ paṭipajjanti,
yathā buddhena desitaṃ;
Dukkhassantaṃ karissanti,
satthu­sāsana­kārino”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
