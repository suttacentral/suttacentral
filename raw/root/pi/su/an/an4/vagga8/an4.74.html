Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

8. Apaṇṇakavagga

74. Paṭha­ma­agga­sutta

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, aggāni. Katamāni cattāri? Sīlaggaṃ, samādhiggaṃ, paññāggaṃ, vimuttaggaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri aggānī”ti.

Catutthaṃ.
