Aṅguttara Nikāya 10

23. Rāgapeyyāla

240–​266 

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, pariññāya … pe … parikkhayāya … pahānāya … khayāya … vayāya … virāgāya … nirodhāya … () cāgāya … paṭinissaggāya … pe … ime dasa dhammā bhāvetabbā”.

Based on the digital edition of the Chaṭṭha Saṅgāyana published by the Vipassana Research Institute, with corrections and proofreading by the Dhamma Society.
