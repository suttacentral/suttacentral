Aṅguttara Nikāya 10

2. Nāthavagga

16. Āhuneyyasutta

“Dasayime, bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjalikaraṇīyā anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassa. Katame dasa? Tathāgato arahaṃ sammāsambuddho, paccekabuddho, ubhato­bhāga­vimutto, paññāvimutto, kāyasakkhī, diṭṭhippatto, saddhāvimutto, saddhānusārī, dhammānusārī, gotrabhū—ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa puggalā āhuneyyā … pe … anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassā”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
