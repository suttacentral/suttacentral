Aṅguttara Nikāya 10

13. Pari­suddha­vagga

131. Navamasutta

“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā ekanta­nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāvimutti—ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā ekanta­nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā”ti.

Navamaṃ.
