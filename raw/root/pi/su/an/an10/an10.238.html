Aṅguttara Nikāya 10

23. Rāgapeyyāla

238 

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya dasa dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame dasa? Aniccasaññā, anattasaññā, āhāre paṭikūlasaññā, sabbaloke anabhi­rata­saññā, aṭṭhikasaññā, puḷavakasaññā, vinīlakasaññā, vipubbakasaññā, vic­chidda­ka­saññā, uddhumā­ta­ka­saññā—rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime dasa dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
