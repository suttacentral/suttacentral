Aṅguttara Nikāya 6

13. Rāgapeyyāla

140 

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya cha dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame cha? Dassa­nā­nuttari­yaṃ, sava­nā­nuttari­yaṃ, lābhā­nuttari­yaṃ, sikkhā­nuttari­yaṃ, pāri­cari­yā­nuttari­yaṃ, anus­sa­tā­nuttari­yaṃ. Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime cha dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
