Aṅguttara Nikāya 6

11. Tikavagga

113. Aratisutta

“Tayome, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame tayo? Arati, vihiṃsā, adhammacariyā. Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo dhammā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṃ dhammānaṃ pahānāya tayo dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame tayo? Aratiyā pahānāya muditā bhāvetabbā, vihiṃsāya pahānāya avihiṃsā bhāvetabbā, adhamma­cari­yāya pahānāya dhammacariyā bhāvetabbā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṃ dhammānaṃ pahānāya ime tayo dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
