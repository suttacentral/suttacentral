Aṅguttara Nikāya 6

10. Ānisaṃsavagga

103. Ukkhittā­si­kasutta

“Cha, bhikkhave, ānisaṃse sampassamānena alameva bhikkhunā sabba­saṅ­khā­resu anodhiṃ karitvā dukkhasaññaṃ upaṭṭhāpetuṃ. Katame cha? ‘Sabba­saṅ­khā­resu ca me nibbidasaññā paccupaṭṭhitā bhavissati, seyyathāpi ukkhittāsike vadhake. Sabbalokā ca me mano vuṭṭhahissati, nibbāne ca santadassāvī bhavissāmi, anusayā ca me samugghātaṃ gacchissanti, kiccakārī ca bhavissāmi, satthā ca me pariciṇṇo bhavissati mettāvatāyā’ti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cha ānisaṃse sampassamānena alameva bhikkhunā sabba­saṅ­khā­resu anodhiṃ karitvā dukkhasaññaṃ upaṭṭhāpetun”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
