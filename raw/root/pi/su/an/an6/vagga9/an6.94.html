Aṅguttara Nikāya 6

9. Sītivagga

94. Tatiya­abhab­baṭ­ṭhāna­sutta

“Chayimāni, bhikkhave, abhab­baṭ­ṭhānāni. Katamāni cha? Abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo mātaraṃ jīvitā voropetuṃ, abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo pitaraṃ jīvitā voropetuṃ, abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo arahantaṃ jīvitā voropetuṃ, abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo tathāgatassa duṭṭhena cittena lohitaṃ uppādetuṃ, abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo saṅghaṃ bhindituṃ, abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo aññaṃ satthāraṃ uddisituṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cha abhab­baṭ­ṭhānānī”ti.

Dasamaṃ.
