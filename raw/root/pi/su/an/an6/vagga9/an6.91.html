Aṅguttara Nikāya 6

9. Sītivagga

91. Abhabbasutta

“Cha, bhikkhave, dhamme abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo uppādetuṃ. Katame cha? Sakkāyadiṭṭhiṃ, vicikicchaṃ, sīlab­bata­parāmā­saṃ, apāyagamanīyaṃ rāgaṃ, apāyagamanīyaṃ dosaṃ, apāyagamanīyaṃ mohaṃ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cha dhamme abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo uppādetun”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
