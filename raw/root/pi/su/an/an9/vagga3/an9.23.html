Aṅguttara Nikāya 9

3. Sattāvāsavagga

23. Taṇhā­mūlaka­sutta

“Nava, bhikkhave, taṇhāmūlake dhamme desessāmi, taṃ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, nava taṇhāmūlakā dhammā? Taṇhaṃ paṭicca pariyesanā, pariyesanaṃ paṭicca lābho, lābhaṃ paṭicca vinicchayo, vinicchayaṃ paṭicca chandarāgo, chandarāgaṃ paṭicca ajjhosānaṃ, ajjhosānaṃ paṭicca pariggaho, pariggahaṃ paṭicca macchariyaṃ, macchariyaṃ paṭicca ārakkho, ārak­khā­dhi­karaṇaṃ daṇḍādānaṃ satthādānaṃ kala­havig­gaha­vivāda­tuvaṃ­tuvaṃ­pe­suñ­ña­musā­vādā aneke pāpakā akusalā dhammā sambhavanti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, nava taṇhāmūlakā dhammā”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
