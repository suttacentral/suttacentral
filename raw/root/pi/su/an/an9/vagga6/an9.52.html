Aṅguttara Nikāya 9

6. Khemavagga

52. Khemasutta

“‘Khemaṃ, kheman’ti, āvuso, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, āvuso, khemaṃ vuttaṃ bhagavatā”ti?

“Idhāvuso, bhikkhu vivicceva kāmehi … pe … paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati. Ettāvatāpi kho, āvuso, khemaṃ vuttaṃ bhagavatā pariyāyena … pe ….

Puna caparaṃ, āvuso, bhikkhu sabbaso neva­saññā­nā­sañ­ñāyata­naṃ samatikkamma saññā­ve­dayi­ta­nirodhaṃ upasampajja viharati, paññāya cassa disvā āsavā parikkhīṇā honti. Ettāvatāpi kho, āvuso, khemaṃ vuttaṃ bhagavatā nippariyāyenā”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
