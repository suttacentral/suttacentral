Aṅguttara Nikāya 9

10. Rāgapeyyāla

94 

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya nava dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame nava? Paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ, dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ, tatiyaṃ jhānaṃ, catutthaṃ jhānaṃ, ākāsānañ­cāyata­naṃ, viñ­ñā­ṇañ­cāyata­naṃ, ākiñ­cañ­ñā­yatanaṃ, neva­saññā­nā­sañ­ñāyata­naṃ, saññā­ve­dayi­ta­nirodho—rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime nava dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
