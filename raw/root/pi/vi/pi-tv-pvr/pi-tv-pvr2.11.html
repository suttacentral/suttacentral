Dutiyabhāga

3. Vipattivāra

Kāyasaṃsaggaṃ ­sādiya­na­pac­cayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ vipattīnaṃ kati vipattiyo bhajanti? Kāyasaṃsaggaṃ ­sādiya­na­pac­cayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ vipattīnaṃ dve vipattiyo bhajanti—siyā sīlavipattiṃ, siyā ācāravipattiṃ … pe …. (1)

Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñ­jana­pac­cayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ vipattīnaṃ kati vipattiyo bhajanti? Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñ­jana­pac­cayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ vipattīnaṃ ekaṃ vipattiṃ bhajanti—ācāravipattiṃ. (2)

Vipattivāro niṭṭhito tatiyo.
