Sekhiyakaṇḍa

Sakkaccavagga

32. Patta­saññī­sikkhā­pada

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anātha­piṇḍi­kassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā piṇḍapātaṃ bhuñjanti, ākirantepi atikkantepi na jānanti … pe ….

“Pattasaññī piṇḍapātaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (32:177)

Pattasaññinā piṇḍapāto bhuñjitabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento piṇḍapātaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

Anāpatti— asañcicca … pe … ādi­kammi­kas­sāti.

Dutiya­sikkhā­padaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
