Sekhiyakaṇḍa

Parimaṇ­ḍala­vagga

8. Dutiya­okkhitta­cak­khu­sikkhā­pada

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anātha­piṇḍi­kassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā antaraghare nisīdanti … pe ….

“Okkhittacakkhu antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (8:153)

Okkhitta­cak­khunā antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ yugamattaṃ pekkhantena. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

Anāpatti—asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādi­kammi­kas­sāti.

Aṭṭha­ma­sikkhā­padaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
