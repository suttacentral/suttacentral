Mātikā

9. Navakauddesa

Nava puggalā—

Sammāsambuddho, pac­ce­ka­sambud­dho, ubhato­bhāga­vimutto, paññāvimutto, kāyasakkhī, diṭṭhippatto, saddhāvimutto, dhammānusārī, saddhānusārī.

Navakaṃ.
