Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

17.31. Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhat­tika

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho nasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ asaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ asaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­asaṃ­ki­lesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­asaṃ­ki­lesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.
