Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

15.48. Cit­ta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­nānupa­rivat­ti­duka, Kusalattika

Cit­ta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­nānupa­rivat­tiṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nocitta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­nā­nupari­vattī nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Cit­ta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­nānupa­rivat­tiṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nocitta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­nā­nupari­vattī naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi. (Abyākatamūlaṃ tīṇiyeva.)
