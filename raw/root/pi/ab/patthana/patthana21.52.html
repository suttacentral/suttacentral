21.52. Mahantaraduka, Sani­dassa­nat­tika

Nasārammaṇaṃ ­nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anārammaṇo sani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

Naanārammaṇaṃ ­nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anārammaṇo sani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

(Ani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghe tīṇiyeva.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nasārammaṇaṃ naani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anārammaṇo ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
