Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (1)

19.15. Ajjhat­tat­tikad­vaya

Naajjhattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca bahiddhā dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

Nabahiddhā dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.

Naaj­jhat­tā­ramma­ṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhat­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

Nabahid­dhā­rammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca bahid­dhā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

Naaj­jhat­tā­ramma­ṇañca nabahid­dhā­rammaṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhat­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā dve … pe … purejāte āsevane dve … pe … avigate cha.
