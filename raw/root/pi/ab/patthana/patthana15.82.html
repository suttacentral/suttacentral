Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

15.82. Saraṇaduka, Parit­tā­ramma­ṇat­tika

Saraṇaṃ parit­tā­ramma­ṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naparit­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Araṇaṃ parit­tā­ramma­ṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naparit­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.

Saraṇaṃ mahagga­tā­rammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nama­hagga­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Araṇaṃ mahagga­tā­rammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nama­hagga­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.

Araṇaṃ appa­māṇā­rammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naappa­māṇā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
