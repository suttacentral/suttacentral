Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

15.76. Saraṇaduka, Pītittika

Saraṇaṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo napītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

Araṇaṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo napītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.

Saraṇaṃ sukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasu­kha­saha­gato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

Araṇaṃ sukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasu­kha­saha­gato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.

Saraṇaṃ upekkhā­saha­gataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naupekkhā­saha­gato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.
