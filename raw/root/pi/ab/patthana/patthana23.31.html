23.31. Parit­tā­ramma­ṇat­tika, Kusalattika

Naparit­tā­ramma­ṇaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā parittārammaṇo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa … pe … avigate ekavīsa.

Naparit­tā­ramma­ṇaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā parittārammaṇo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cuddasa … pe … avigate cuddasa.

Naparit­tā­rammaṇo naabyākato dhammo parit­tā­ramma­ṇassa abyākatassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) … Ārammaṇe ekavīsa, adhipatiyā nava, anantare samanantare ekavīsa, upanissaye ekavīsa, natthiyā vigate ekavīsa.
