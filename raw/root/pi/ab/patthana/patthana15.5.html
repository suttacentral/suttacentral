Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

15.5. Hetuduka, Saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhat­tika

Hetuṃ saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nahetuṃ asaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Hetuṃ asaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­asaṃ­ki­lesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­asaṃ­ki­lesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
