Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīya (2), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

8.41. Cit­tānupa­rivat­ti­duka

Nacit­tānupa­rivat­tiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacit­tānupa­rivatti dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.
