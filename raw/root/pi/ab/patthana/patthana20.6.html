Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

20.6. Upādā­na­goc­chaka

Naupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca upādāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
