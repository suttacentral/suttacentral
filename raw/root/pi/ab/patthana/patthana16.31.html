Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

16.31. Pītittika, Hetuduka

Pītisahagataṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca napītisahagato nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā aṭṭhavīsa … pe … avigate aṭṭhavīsa.

Pītisahagataṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupekkhā­saha­gato nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā aṭṭhārasa … pe … avigate aṭṭhārasa.
