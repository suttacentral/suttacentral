Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

23.35. Uppannattikādi, Kusalattika

Nauppanno nakusalo dhammo uppannassa kusalassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. Ārammaṇe satta, adhipatiyā cha, upanissaye satta.

Nauppanno naakusalo dhammo uppannassa akusalassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. Ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā cha, upanissaye cha.

Naanupanno naabyākato dhammo uppannassa abyākatassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo … tīṇi.

Nauppanno naabyākato dhammo uppannassa abyākatassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe satta, adhipatiyā cattāri, sahajāte nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye satta … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Atītattikaṃ uppannat­ti­ka­sadi­saṃ.
