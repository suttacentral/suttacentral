Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (1)

13.18. Atītattika

Paccuppanno dhammo naatītassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava … pe … upanissaye nava … pe … avigate tīṇi.
