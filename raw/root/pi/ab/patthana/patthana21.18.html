Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

21.18. Hetuduka, Vipākattika

Nahetuṃ navipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu vipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nahetuṃ ­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammaṃ paccayā hetu vipā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nahetuṃ naneva­vipāka­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu neva­vipāka­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
