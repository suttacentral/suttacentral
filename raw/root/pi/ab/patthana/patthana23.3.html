Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

23.3. Kusalattika, Upādinnattika

Nakusalo naupādin­nu­pādā­niyo dhammo abyākatassa upādin­nu­pādā­niyassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo … cha pañhā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe cha, anantare samanantare upanissaye cha, purejāte tīṇi, kamme pañca, āhāre tīṇi, atthiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nakusalaṃ naanupā­din­nu­pādā­niyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato anupā­dinnu­pādā­niyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate pañca.

Nakusalaṃ naa­nupādin­na­a­nu­pādā­niyaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo anupādin­na­anupā­dā­niyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe cha … pe … āsevane tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
