3.2.2.1.1. Anulomapuggala

» Yassa cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhati tassa sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhatīti?

Sacakkhukānaṃ asotakānaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhati, no ca tesaṃ sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhati. Sacakkhukānaṃ sasotakānaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ ­cak­khā­ya­tanañca nirujjhati sotāyatanañca nirujjhati.

« Yassa vā pana sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhati tassa cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhatīti?

Sasotakānaṃ acakkhukānaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhati, no ca tesaṃ cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhati. Sasotakānaṃ sacakkhukānaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ sotāyatanañca nirujjhati ­cak­khā­ya­tanañca nirujjhati.
